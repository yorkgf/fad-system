十一　Northwestern University 西北大学
学生背景
小赵同学兴趣广泛，文理兼修。他虽然热爱工程，却又不像别的孩子那样整天埋头于模型，而是将自己对工程的热爱倾注于生活。除了工程，经济学也是小赵喜欢的专业。在一次实践中，他通过对中国蓬勃发展的线上旅游市场的个案分析和调查，研究了携程网的经营策略、存在的问题，并有针对性地提出了自己的解决方案。此次调查，不仅测试了他所学习的理论框架和方法，还展示了他的特殊经历，又表明他有相当的相关方面的知识背景。除此之外，小赵同学还广泛发展自己各方面的兴趣。这样一个性格多样化又学以致用、勇于创新的学生无疑完全符合西北大学自由、革新、活跃的办学理念。
案例
题目1
What are the unique qualities of Northwestern—and of the specific undergraduate school to which you are applying to attend the University? In what ways do you hope to take advantage of the qualities you have identified?
西北大学以及你申请的本科学院有什么独特气质吸引了你？你希望如何利用它？
分析
坐落在密歇根湖畔的美国西北大学，建校一百多年，是当之无愧的名牌大学。其商学院曾在1988年至1994年，六年蝉联美国各名牌大学商学院的排名第一，是莘莘学子争相报考的学院。
题目中问到，西北大学有什么特质，以及具体哪个学院吸引了你，这就要求申请该校的学生在提交申请之前对该校有所了解。了解的方式很多，可以通过网络资料，也可以通过朋友、老师，还可以自己亲身去学校感受。无论什么方式，你要明白你即将申请的是一所什么样的学校。就大学本身而言，不仅要招收优质生源，还要招到最合适的生源。
另外要注意的是，写这篇文章，不仅要回答出特质是什么，还要答出这样的特质对你有什么样的影响，你将如何利用这些特质来让自己成才，这些特质在你未来的道路上将有何作用。
范文
开篇点出西北大学开放、自由、力求与人交往的学风以及学以致用的精神。
I love almost everything about Northwestern, and what Northwestern particularly attracts me is its open, liberal and cultivating spirit towards students and the closely tied bonds between classes and the practical world beyond the campus.
本段开篇说明自己将要申请的学院，点出西北大学跨学科教学的特色，而这正是我所看重的特质，继而对西北大跨学科教学这一教学特色做出进一步阐释，并谈到了自己可能的学习计划和梦想中的大学，最后谈到西北大学可能给予自己的名望和学识是每一个西北大学学子一生的财富。
I plan to apply for the Robert R. McCormick School of Engineering and Applied Science and major in Applied Mathematics. In Northwestern, walls or divisions among departments and schools only exist in its material terms, but never in academic pursuit or interdisciplinary pursuit. McCormick is never so far away from Kellogg, and engineering walks closer to finance in Northwestern. This vigorous heartbeat of Northwestern holds particular magic to me. I like such a vibrant, diversified and cross-boundary learning environment, Only with such a campus that encourages exploration and adventures across the lines will I be able to expose myself to as many subjects, thoughts and ways as possible. Isn't this the true spirit of liberal education? I want to take science courses in the morning, marketing research seminar in the afternoon, and maybe a little later, sit in a lecture given by some famous Northwesterner on how solar power company structure impacts on the whole patenting process. My dreamed education in college is a lively process, not a dead scheduled curriculum. The name of Northwestern itself is an intangible asset to us, standing for science development, social responsibility, elite citizenship and knowledge. Now my life in Northwestern may seem like a beautiful dream, but I am sure I will live that dream myself very soon.
分析
本文表面看来朴实无华，却恰到好处地回答了题目中的所有问题，而这也正是校方想通过报考学生对该题的回答所想要得到的结果。回答这样的题目，你不必在文字和句式上花费过多的工夫，太过华丽可能会适得其反。
文章开宗明义，言明西北大学的特点和自己将要申请的学院，然后进一步阐释这些特点，并表达了作者心目中理想的大学形象。最后谈到大学可能带给个人的影响，这些都无形中契合了题目所提出的要求。
题目2
Optional Essay
分析
这里的选择回答文书希望申请人提供自己做过的或正在进行的学术文章、论文或者科研题目。
范文
在过去六个月的课程中，我通过对中国蓬勃发展的线上旅游市场的个案分析和调查，研究了企业的经营策略。该研究项目采用了主要和次要的研究方法，测试了我所学习的理论框架和方法。
Over the course of the last six months, I have researched business strategy by investigating and analyzing an embattled player in China's thriving online travel market. This project has allowed me to pursue primary and secondary research avenues as a test of my learned theoretical frames and approaches.
本段介绍了自己的研究对象“携程网”，并写明研究目的——找出恢复市场份额的方法，并确保其长期盈利。
Founded in 1999, Ctrip is currently the dominant online travel agency in China, with more than 50% of market share in the industry. A NASDAQ-listed enterprise that offers airline and hotel bookings, packaged tours and corporate travel arrangements, Ctrip targets primarily the middle-class customers who take up the bulk of these services. In recent quarters, however, Ctrip's position on the sector has begun to slip, as new market entrants and no-commission, vertical-search substitute competition successfully for suppliers and clients. I set out to discover what steps Ctrip should take to regain its market share and ensure its long-term profitability.
描述了具体的研究方法，一是采访携程网的公关宣传部负责人；二是做出书面报告，截取60位顾客的网上旅游预订需求。然后提出携程网的问题：携程不太可能打赢价格战。它的战略需要更多的创意和灵活，而不是简单地重新跟供应商谈判或提供折扣。
To gain as full a picture as possible, I drew my information from a variety of sources. First, I secured an interview with head of Ctrip's Public Relations department to learn what approaches Ctrip has been taking as response to the changing market conditions. His input confirmed that Ctrip provides a broader range of services than many of its competitors, a potential area to exploit in distinguishing the Ctrip brand from others. At present, however, it offers each service only on an a-la-carte basis. I then conducted a written survey, eliciting the views of 60 randomly chosen individuals about their online travel booking needs. From that data, I found that Ctrip has been unable to differentiate itself through price: the public perceives that it offers services similar to other companies at roughly the same cost to consumers. Indeed, my market research, which I conducted through secondary sources, demonstrated that Ctrip is unlikely to be able to win a price war. Its strategy needs to be more creative and flexible than simply renegotiating supplier deals or offering spot discounts.
得出研究结论：改革其业务模式。并就携程网运营存在的问题提出三点改进建议。一是公司重心从中档客户转移到高端客户，二是使用酒店和度假村帮助携程网制定差异化产品，三是向国际客户拓展。
Based upon all of the evidence I gathered, I concluded that Ctrip needs to overhaul its business model in a very strategic way. I recommend that (1) the company shift its focus from midrange to higher-end customers and combine its services into comprehensive packages that better cater to those consumers' travel preferences. I believe this approach would allow it to corner up to 80% of revenue in the market by targeting people who travel frequently and access multiple services each time they do. (2)Alliances with more exclusive hotels and resorts could also help Ctrip design differentiated products that would appeal to the wealthy, such as packages including fine dining or limousine services, thus enhancing its reputation among this clientele. (3) And the outreach to international customers is also expected to generate an entirely new source of revenue largely untapped by other players in the marketplace.
表明自己大学想要学习的专业，并希望能在西北大学学习。
The report, now a draft form awaiting feedback from my teacher, is expected to be delivered to the Ctrip executives upon completion of revision. I have embraced the independence that this assignment has afforded. As a result, I intend to make individual research a priority during my undergraduate studies, hopefully with the aid of Northwestern's expert business faculty.
分析
这篇选答文章详细介绍了作者的一次市场调研和分析过程。本文既展示了作者特殊的经历，又表明作者有相当的相关方面的知识背景。这样给人的印象就是这名学生在商业方面是一个可造之才，这无疑给录取人员留下了好的印象，也会给自己的申请加分。
文章的写作调理清晰，结构分明，没有多余的话语，语言也相对平实，但这并不影响给作者加分。
