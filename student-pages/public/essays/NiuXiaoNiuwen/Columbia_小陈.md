题目5
Short Answer Questions
Please tell us what you found meaningful about one of the above mentioned books, publications or cultural events. Characters available 1,500
你在上面提到的图书、报刊或文化事件中，你认为哪个是有意义的？（1500字符）
分析
什么是有意义？这也许是动笔前我们最应该问问自己的。
事物意义的大小表示了人对事物的情感重视的程度，事物本身有存在的价值但没有意义的存在，意义只是人对事物的一种情感赋予，这种赋予的情感在事物身上的体现就是意义的来源，任何事物的意义都是由人的情感决定的，一切事物意义的存在都是人的感情上的存在，这种存在的基本意义是为有无的意义。
所以，但凡你认为书也好杂志也罢，抑或是某件事件本身，如果它带给了你思考启迪，或是反思引起了你的共鸣，我们就能说它是有意义的。
范文
我喜欢《枪炮、病菌与钢铁》一书，它带给我一个全新的视角去看待大自然，或人类本身如何去改变人类历史。
I like the book "Gun, Germ and Steel" by Jared Diamond. Always passionate about liberal science subjects, I find the book informative and enlightening in that it gives me a broadened and renewed perspective of how the path of human history could be changed by some objectified forces, or even some monstrous forces created by ourselves.
我也喜欢《追风筝的人》，它反映了阿富汗人民不同寻常的一面，他们所受的折磨、痛苦以及他们的愿望和激情。
I also read "the Kite Runner" several months ago, and found that is a touching piece of work. When the War in Afghanistan verges to an end, people's lives there may have been changed for the worse. This book sheds lights on the different aspects of Afghan people, their torments, their sufferings, their aspirations and their passions. This book is by all means a great reflection of idealism, humanism and consequences of the inevitability.
分析
作者提到了两本自己认为有意义的书，第一本是《枪炮、病菌与钢铁》，通过这本书，作者明白了大自然或人类本身如何去改变人类历史。第二本是《追风筝的人》，作者通过这本书看到了阿富汗人民所受的折磨、痛苦以及他们的愿望和激情，并从中看出了理想主义，人文主义和后果的必然性。
题目6
Please tell us what academic class has been your favorite and why. Characters available 1,500
请告诉我们哪个科目是你一直最喜欢的，为什么。（1500字符）
分析
你需要通过描述你喜欢的科目去发掘你的喜好、性格和潜能。
范文
我最喜欢高三下半学期的欧洲历史课，之前的阅读都是表面的，只是通过数字或是官方给出的观点来评价历史。通过潘老师的讲解，我学会了从当时人物的角度来批判是非对错。独立思考是潘老师教会我们的最宝贵的东西。
My favorite class is the European history class in last term of my high school. Since we were not pressured by the final Chinese College Entrance Exams, the instructor, Mr. Pan, started to deliver something I believe to be real stuff in the lecture. Before this class, all of my historical views were rather distorted, that is evaluating and gouging historical events or figures through a pair of glasses that have been pre-set by authorities. The criteria of such rigid or inflexible reading of history are surprisingly modern, using the current standards to judge things and events a thousand years ago. It sounds like an epistemological paradox, as we are inevitably using our modern eyes to see what happened in history. But Mr. Pan's lecture was as stimulating as it is provocative to many, which proposed to read the history with a standard conforming as closely as possible to the then existing standards. "Did he tell me to act or think like a 17th century Frenchman now?" I started to question the book, him, myself, and everything. If French revolution, scrutinized with then existing viewpoint, is by all means a rebellion that torments the nation, while in comparison, England, adopting the relatively peaceful constitutional monarchy, enjoys tranquility, development and harmony. Confusion flooded me at first as I was struggling, but later, it became all so much clear to me. Independent thinking, later I realized, was the gist that Mr. Pan tried to informed us with in that class.
分析
哪是最喜欢的科目，作者给出的答案是欧洲历史课。通过这个科目，作者系统掌握了当时的欧洲风情，还学会了用批判性思维去客观评价当时的改革或是战争。这篇文章条理清晰，更为难能可贵的是，作者通过试举法国革命和英国政变两例来说明“I started to question the book, him, myself, and everything.”质疑的能力。书上说的东西都是正确的吗？老师说过的话都是至理名言吗？这样的写作风格无疑增加了可读性，让录取委员会的人眼前一亮。
题目7
Please tell us what you find most appealing about Columbia and why. Characters available 1,500
你认为哥伦比亚最吸引你的地方是什么？（1500字符）
分析
每个学校都喜欢问这个问题。他们似乎非常喜欢通过这个问题来了解你的诚意，看你是否真心想来这个学校。正如我在前面分析中提到的一样，一个极佳的答案一定是非常具体的，聚焦于一个特点来写出哥伦比亚的特殊之处。此外，写作前，请尽可能从网上详细地搜索些跟这所学校有关的你感兴趣的领域，看看他们在该领域能够给你提供什么。
范文
一座城市、一个人、一个学校让我选择了哥伦比亚。我之前写过很多城市，但纽约市跟我之前描述过的所有城市都不一样。
One city, one person and one school, are exactly why I choose Columbia. This summer I traveled to the Big Apple for UN Youth Summit and fell in love with New York. It is a colorful mix, to me, of multiple facets, various flavors, complex feels and styles, embracing the future and the past. I wrote about cities a lot, but NYC is not like any one of those I described before.
我的目标是成为像本杰明·格雷厄姆（第一个引进证券分析概念）那样的人，那么我需要跟他一样的教育。于我而言，教育是一种信仰，一种信念，和对生活的癫狂。
One person also takes up a special position in my heart. Benjamin Graham was the name, and he is a Columbia graduate. He opened a new age of accurate and refined securities analysis, combining effectively a shrewd examination of the regulatory environment, corporation and insider behaviors instead of simply one Dow Jones. His name was so shining that it never walks out of my head ever since. To become such a figure like Benjamin, one needs to start from taking the same education. A person is shaped and molded by his own education. I started to admire Benjamin's own alma mater more than anything else. Education is a faith, a belief, or an obsession in life.
所以我想到了哥伦比亚，当年本杰明接受教育的地方。我在哥伦比亚校园看到很多精力充沛、充满活力的学生。哥伦比亚的人是吸引我选择在这里读大学的最重要的原因。
One school, Columbia then walks into my head ever since and I believe doing the same education as does Benjamin will be my most audacious try in my life so far. Again, I met a group of energetic and vibrant people on Columbia campus. It is not the Low Library, the Alma Mater, or the great Morning Side campus that mesmerizes me. The people, always the people, of Columbia community count for the most important reason I choose here as my college.
分析
"One city, one person and one school, are exactly why I choose Columbia”“一座城市、一个人、一个学校让我选择了哥伦比亚。”这种开门见山的方式让人一看就知道作者的结构分布，短短一两分钟就能明白作者想表达的中心，而这也是录取委员会的老师喜欢的。试想，他们每天要阅览上千份的申请，分到每篇文章的时间也就一两分钟。所以简洁明了永远是最重要的，让读者知道你在讲什么。
One city, one person and one school便是作者要讲的。One city指的是作者曾经在联合国青年峰会期间拜访过的地方。它的多姿多彩、变化无穷的风格让作者着迷。One person指的是本杰明·格雷厄姆，一个在金融方面做出过巨大贡献的人，作者以他为榜样，想成为他那样的人。One school则说的是哥伦比亚，本杰明的母校，校园充满着充满活力的学生。作者非常希望能成为他们中的一员。
题目8
For applicants to Columbia College, please tell us what from your current and past experiences (either academic or personal) attracts you specifically to the field or fields of study that you noted in the Application Data section. If you are currently undecided, please write about any field or fields in which you may have an interest at this time. Characters available 1,500.
请告诉我们你感兴趣的领域。如若你已有决定，请从你过去的经历出发讲讲它是怎么吸引的你；如若没有决定，请说说对于此刻而言你感兴趣的专业。（1500字符）
分析
对于自己感兴趣的领域，每个人都可以说上一二。说出来是简单的，但要说好同样考验一个人的能力。好的作答要学会将自己的兴趣跟所报学校相联系，让录取委员会的人认为这个学生是他们的不二人选。下面的例文也许可以给你些启示。
范文
化学家和莎士比亚、物理学家宾汉和足球选手雷纳、电动力学和神学这些看似不挨边的组合在哥伦比亚大学却是紧密联系在了一起。因为哥伦比亚是一个培养全才的地方。
Life is full of surprises and I care for almost everything in life. There are no two extremes in my dictionary. Chemist and Shakespeare, Bingham and Reiner, Electrodynamics and Theology, these words are really strange combinations for many, but they are close knitted here, a University where science and engineering join hands perfectly. I think Columbia is a place close to "omniscience" and the Columbia people know "everything flows" and can better understand "Subtle Is the Lord, but malicious He is not." What a fascinating palace that Creation and Renovation flow through!
我同时喜欢历史和经济。历史记录了我们这个世界，而经济将人类活动转化成现实的应用。在高中我负责运营一个大的社团，给其他部门提供资金支持。正因为之前的经历，我对这两个学科有着极为浓厚的兴趣，尤其当它们两个结合的时候。
I love history and economics at the same time. They are in my blood. Not surprisingly, my passion for history actually grows from my love of reading. History records the very core elements and actions of our world, and economics, building upon such constitution, transforms such elements and actions into actual applications in our world. Historians abstract the pass, present and memories of our world, while economists rationalize and realize such behaviors. As I look at it, historians and economists share the dreams. I have practiced some "economical" projects in my high school year, to run a large student club that helps secure the external funding for other student associations. This idea was drawing wisdom from my history readings on rapid market development in early Ming Dynasty. It was little crazy that I managed to collect enough money to afford my interest by doing small business trades in school. The success in the organizations and little trades promotes me to learn more things regarding history and economics. Thus I became more fascinated by the two subjects, especially when they are combined organically.
分析
作者喜欢历史和经济。对于大多数的学校来说，只可能满足作者其中的一个需求。但哥伦比亚大学不一样，它是一个培养全才的地方，并且还注重创新和改革。值得一提的是，文中还用到哥伦比亚的校训“Subtle Is the Lord, but malicious He is not.”可以看出作者写作之前是仔细了解了这个学校的。
第二段，作者特别将历史和经济进行对比说明：Historians abstract the pass, present and memories of our world, while economists rationalize and realize such behaviors. 之后还特举一例解释说明这两者相辅相成的作用，强有力的说明了自己为什么喜欢这两个专业。
题目9
For applicants to The Fu Foundation School of Engineering and Applied Science, please tell us from your current and past experiences (either academic or personal) what attracts you specifically to the study of engineering. Characters available 1,500
如果申请“华富基金”工程与应用科学学院，请告诉我们你为什么想就读工程专业，有过怎样的经历。（1500字符）
分析
对于绝大多数学校来讲，要想申请工程专业，申请者必须多写一篇文章来表明为什么对这个专业感兴趣。这篇文章其实也很简单，但请记住一定要把经历说清楚。真实可信是重中之重。不是说不能去夸大，适当夸大是可以的，但一定要掌握好这个度，让录取委员会的人读来是信得过你的。你可以讲讲你深入阅读一本书的经历，也可以是受父亲的影响接触到了这个行业领域并因此有了深厚的兴趣。自己去挖掘属于自己的故事，这样才能做到与众不同。
