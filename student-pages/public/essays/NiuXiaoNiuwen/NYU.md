十　New York University 纽约大学
学生背景
小李来自太原。她虽然生性安静，做事却非常有主见，喜欢独自行动。生活中有一件事情对她产生过消积的影响，那就是由于老师曾经骂过她，以至于造成了心理阴影，产生了轻微的结巴。她花了近五年的时间才走出来，其间中学的一个同桌给了她很大的帮助。这件事让她认识到，很多时候我们不能抱怨世界的不公，而是应该勇敢面对它，锻炼自己、依靠自己。
案例
题目1
Campus: NYU New York
Tell us why you have chosen the above campus (using a maximum of 700 characters—spaces and punctuation included).
你为什么会选择纽约大学？（包括空格和标点最多700字符）
范文
选择纽约大学首先是学术上的成就，其次是地理位置优越，除此之外，学生来自不同的国家，有着文化的多样性。
Its excellence in academy attracts me most. NYU is famous for its academic performance. Prominent scholars work here. They are characterized by collaboration, innovation and incandescent teaching. From the newspaper and Internet, I know there are many prize winners among NYU graduates. I'm not surprised because such a notable university will certainly bring us elites to contribute a to better world. Additionally, this university locates in one of the most influential cities. Students from different countries will reflect their diversity in culture, ways of thinking and so on. This diversified environment makes student life in NYU remarkable and interesting.
题目2
Personal Statements
SHORT-ANSWER ESSAYS
Please respond to each
 of the following questions using a maximum of 1,500 characters (spaces and punctuation included) in the space provided.
请回答下面的每一个问题。包括空格和标点最多1500字符。
◎问题1
NYU's global network provides students with hundreds of academic areas of interest for students to cultivate their intellectual curiosity and to help achieve their career goals. Whether you are entirely undecided about your academic plans or you have a definitive program of study in mind, what are your own academic interests? Feel free to share any thoughts on any particular programs or how you might explore those interests at NYU on any of our campuses.
不管你是否已经决定就读某一专业，请说说对于此刻而言，你感兴趣的领域。
分析
对于自己感兴趣的领域，每个人都可以说上一二。说出来简单，但要说好却考验一个人的能力。好的作答要学会将自己的兴趣跟所选学校匹配，让录取委员会的人认为这个学生是他们的不二人选。
范文
我感兴趣的领域是社会工作和公众福利。目前社会面临的问题很多，诸如贫困儿童上学问题、老年人养老院问题。人生而平等，我想要为人权而做出贡献。
To be honest, what really attracts me is NYU's focus in the social work. I'm interested in social work and public welfare. And the Silver School of social work could provide me such an opportunity to learn more professional knowledge about healthcare, human rights and so on. I also learn from the internet that NYU will concern about the most pressing social problems. And NYU has the most comprehensive curriculum in the social work area. Nowadays, there are many social problems in different countries. For example, children from rural area or developing countries do not have the opportunity to study as children from rich families or developed countries. Older people in some countries can not be taken care of in nursing houses. I believe that human are born to be equal. Their benefit must not be deprived by others. We need make efforts to fight for their human rights and contribute to a better world. Thus, I will participate in activities in these areas and I will try my best to master my major.
分析
文章作答凝练，200字的回答清楚明了。作者首段便提出自己的兴趣领域，在末尾提到喜欢的原因在于社会面临的问题很多，而我想去拥有一个更好的世界。除此之外，作者还将纽约大学与其兴趣领域匹配，认为纽约大学能够给她机会去学习专业知识，进而为她的梦想起到推波助澜的作用。
◎问题2
What intrigues you? Tell us about one work of art, scientific achievement, piece of literature, method of communication, or place in the world (a film, book, performance, website, event, location, etc.), and explain its significance to you.
什么东西让你感兴趣？告诉我们一个艺术作品、科学成就、文学作品、交流方式或地方（比如电影、图书、表演、网站、事件、地点等），并向我们解释它的重要性。
分析
这里说的作品，并不是指畅销书或心灵鸡汤一类的浅显无深意的东西。如果你之前用了大量时间来阅读学术方面或让你深入思考的书，那将其作为话题是不错的选择；还有譬如能引起你思考的电影，诸如《肖申克的救赎》、《沉默的羔羊》等；表演方面像朗朗的钢琴演奏会；事件如2008年北京奥运会……总的说来，选择的主题一定是你感兴趣的，并从中有思考有感悟有收获的。这样写出来的文章肯定阅读性很强。
范文
《简·爱》是我最喜欢的小说，女主人公简·爱同时也是我的偶像。她是一位独立勇敢并充满了乐观精神的女性。我从中得到启发：我应该看到生活积极的一面，就像简一样，因为黑暗终会消失在阳光下。
The book Jane Eyre is one of my favorite books and the heroine Jane Eyre is my idol. As far as I'm concerned, she is an independent and brave female full of optimistic attitude. In the novel, she left her family members and studied in the boarding school. But she never gave in to the difficulty of life. Jane managed to change her life and became a tutor. Then, she can change others' lives. This reminds me of my own life. I need to be independent in a total different world and I need to be strong. These traits of Jane Eyre would be a lesson to me. Facing difficulty, I cannot cry, or give up, but smile and persist. Life is full of joy and hope. I should see the positive side of life just like what Jane has done, because darkness will finally disappear under the sunshine.
此外，她对男女平等、贫富平等的坚持也让我赞赏。简·爱能勇敢地对抗偏见、经受住考验。
In addition, her insistence in the equality between poor and rich, female and male is what I highly appreciate. "Do you think, because I am poor, obscure, plain, and little, I am soulless and heartless? You think wrong!—I have as much soul as you—and full as much heart!" These sentences strongly aroused my resonance when I read them. I admired Jane Eyre who is brave to fight against the prejudices and is strong to weather the storm she experienced.
分析
作者非常喜欢《简·爱》里面的女主人公简·爱，因为她是一位独立勇敢的女性，还因为她对男女平等、贫富平等的坚持。看完《简·爱》，作者受到了极大地震撼，并从中得到了能量与鼓舞。她从中明白了要笑对挫折，积极地去面对生活。
