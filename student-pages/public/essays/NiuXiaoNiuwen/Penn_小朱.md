六　University of Pennsylvania 宾夕法尼亚大学
学生背景
小朱同学来自上海，喜欢学习工程类。他在高二时转学去了美国，之后一直没有放弃对科学实验的热爱。他初中的时候自己做过一个机器人，但以失败告终。这次失败对他影响很深，但这件事没有在文书中体现。另外一件事是一次模型比赛，他发现国内的这种比赛有时候是凭运气的，变动因素很大。他参加的最有创意的一次比赛是学校举行的“科学之夜”。用20克木头搭成架子，看谁能承受的最重。结果他拿了比赛的第一名。这个比赛让他很吃惊，他没有想到20克之轻竟然能承受如此之重。科学奇迹是可能发生的。这件事还是很有意义的。他SAT分数2100，在这里文书就起了拔高的作用。
案例
题目1
ESSAYS (REQUIRED OF ALL APPLICANTS)
Short Answer:
A Penn education provides a liberal arts and sciences foundation across multiple disciplines with a practical emphasis in one of four undergraduate schools: the College of Arts and Sciences, the School of Engineering and Applied Science, the School of Nursing, or the Wharton School.
宾夕法尼亚大学提供了横跨多个学科的文科和理科基础专业。文理学院、工程及应用科学学院、护理学院、沃顿商学院都有其应用课程。
Given the undergraduate school to which you are applying, please discuss why you choose Penn as your academic destination. (Please answer in 300 words or less.)
请讲讲你为什么会选择宾夕法尼亚大学。（300字以内）
分析
其实宾夕法尼亚大学的强势在于研究领域及跨学科研究领域，所以如果你有这方面的研究背景或是对跨学科研究感兴趣，一定要在文中提到。我们当中肯定有不少人在进大学时都不知道对哪科感兴趣，这都无所谓。但一定要记住，如果你基于以往的经验有明确的目标，不管是实际经验也好，深入阅读也罢，在这篇文章中展现你的学术投入是一个明智的决定。
如果你拜访过宾夕法尼亚大学，或是参加过宾大在中国的讲座，回想一下你最感兴趣的地方。如果你认识里面的同学再好不过，可以问问他们最喜欢学校的什么东西。花一些时间想象你置身于校园中，将学校最吸引你的地方通过这篇文章告诉给录取委员会的人。同样，你也可以说说你过往的经验，讲讲你可以怎样去为宾夕法尼亚大学做出贡献。
范文
开头就提出自己申请的原因：经历和兴趣使然。
Taking my experience and interest into consideration, I am going to apply the School of Engineering and Applied Science, one of the most prestigious school of University of Pennsylvania since its establishment.
宾夕法尼亚大学在工程与应用科学方面独领风骚，能提供很全面的课程。同时，学校里面有很多有经验的教授和学术前沿的第一手资料，学生科研方面也提供了丰富的基金。另外，来自不同国家的优秀人才集聚于此，有益于培养我的国际视野。
As a leading one in this field, School of Engineering and Applied Science provides a well-rounded curriculum, forming an even more comprehensive pool of knowledge and expertise for me. Therefore, it's of great importance for me to take the courses seriously so that I can master basic knowledge solidly, laying a foundation for the deeper research or future career. I like this school as it has assembled the resources for its undergraduate students to address the growing need for skilled professionals while adhering to its high standards of academic discovery. Courses given by famous professors make it available for me to share my thoughts with them and get the access to the academic front-line. Moreover, it has put much emphasis on the funds support for students' research like Bridge Funding from Penn Engineering, which also stimulates me to make some a difference in academic research. I especially admire for the Summer Undergraduate Research in Engineering, such a precious opportunity for me to be instructed by world acclaimed faculty hands-in-hands. Meanwhile, top minds from different countries attract me since it will largely contribute to the formation of my international perspective, and also, help me to learn from the greatest minds in the world.
在宾大，我希望学业上获得很高的成就。我知道这需要付出很多汗水、精力和思想动力，我也知道我已准备好面对这些挑战。
In University of Pennsylvania, I want to achieve high for my future study. I understand that it will take a lot of sweat, energy and mind-power, and I know I am ready to face that challenge.
分析
文章首段中作者表示希望进入工程与应用科学学院学习。接下来，作者通过连接词的使用(Moreover…Meanwhile…)和句与句之间的内部联系（Applied Science provides…I like this school as…），向录取委员会的人陈述了四点理由：全面的课程、优秀的老师、充足的资金和不同社会背景的精英。最后一段，表示虽然会有挫折苦难，但“我”有信心去面对！
题目2
Essay:
Ben Franklin once said, "All mankind is divided into three classes: those that are immovable, those that are movable, and those that move.”
本杰明·富兰克林曾经说过：“所有的人可分为三类：一类永远不变，一类可以改变，还有一类一直在变。”
Which are you? (Please answer in 300-500 words.)
你是哪一类人？（字数300～500）
分析
这类题目一直不好处理，申请的同学看到后的第一反应往往是发懵，继而是犹豫良久难以下笔。这样的问题其实没有所谓的正确答案。你可以单写其中的一类，也可以把它们结合在一起写。毕竟人是多样的，在某一方面能体现出他的变化性，而另一方面则体现出他的不变性。
Immovable（永远不变）方面比如：有人信佛或信基督教，我想在这件事上，对于有信仰的人来说，这是支撑他们活下去的动力，很难改变。
Movable（可以改变）方面比如：对于某件事或某个人的看法，也许在历经了一次劫难之后会突然有所改变；也或者在与别人交往的过程中，学会去改变以前固有的恶习，虽然无奈，但还是决心改变。
Move方面：这里讲的改变是指一直在变，一直在不断像水一般调整自己的方向。这样的人，我们说会更容易融入新的环境中，更易于与他人打交道。
其实不管是哪类，只要写出你内心的独白，你的经历体会，都会是上乘之作。
范文
我是那种一直在改变的人。我一直提醒自己做到积极、乐观和勤奋，而且我也抓住各种机会让自己全面发展。
As matter of fact, I believe that I am definitely among those that move. I have kept reminding myself to be active, impressive, optimistic and hardworking. With a keen sense, I cherish every opportunity to improve myself in an all-around way, with the goal to be a useful person chasing my dream and at the same time, contributing to the world at large.
我就读高中的学校很好，在那里我拓宽了自己的视野，感受了不同的社会文化。此外，我有着良好的适应力和交流能力，大家都能很好地接受我。
I felt so lucky that I spent my high school in FD High School, where I largely broadened my horizon and experienced a totally different cultural diversity. In a foreign country, despite of hardships and difficulties, I always encouraged myself to be active in communication and participation. FD High School gathers students from all over the world, enabling me to communicate with friend from different cultures and gradually, I learnt to show great respect for diverse cultures, different customs and even religious beliefs by close contact. Moreover, I have found that classes, with emphasis on active interaction, are interesting and informative, getting students involved in the whole process rather than passive acceptance. With strong adaptability and excellent communication skill, I was totally accepted by the campus community.
刚到学校我就参加了越野比赛。第一天我奇迹般地获得了第三的成绩。此外我还帮助别人并赢得了大家的尊重，在这一过程中我完全融进了大家的圈子。
When I firstly arrived at FD High School, I competed for Cross Country Running, a race held by our school for newcomers. At the beginning, I was totally ignored because almost no one believed that a Chinese could finish this in a good result. At the moment, I kept silence and decided to prove myself in real actions. In the first day, I surprisingly ranked the third, making a stir on the campus. Furthermore, in the race, I helped some exhausted students and won their respect and trust. Finally, I achieved a good result, but more importantly, I found I have completely blended in the community in the course of sweat and difficulty.
这个夏天，我在苏州博物馆实习。在实习过程中，我对石桥有了浓厚的兴趣。之后物理课堂上的设计比赛中，老师要求我们设计一个20克重的桥梁，赢家则为设计承载能力最大的一位。我绞尽脑汁，最终设计了一个以石桥作为原型的样板。因其承载重量是30公斤，我最终赢得了一等奖。
This summer, I interned in Suzhou Museum, where I experienced the intellectual move in a very innovative way. Suzhou Museum, a classic combination of the traditional and modern architectural styles, reflects the wisdom of Chinese People in every corner. During this internship, I developed my great interest and passion for bridges, especially stone bridges that without any steel or concrete can bear hundreds of times as heavy as its own weight withstanding centuries of countless winds and rains. This experience exerted profound influence on my later studies. In Science Night, a design competition held in my high school physics class, every student was required to design a 20 gram-weight bridge and the winner would be coming from the designer whose bridge could bear the largest weight. I racked my brains and finally chose a stone bridge in Suzhou Museum as my prototype model. Through strict demonstrations, studies and frequent consultancy from experts in Suzhou Museum, I eventually made it. As a matter of course, I won the first prize due to that my bridge could bear 30 kilogram-weight.
Should I continue to move mentally and physically? Definitely a resounding YES.
分析
全文的构思非常巧妙。全文通篇讲述自己一直在变的能力——思想上的、身体上的、智力上的。第一段总述全文，讲明自己一直希望全面提高自己。接下来作者讲述自己初到美国读高中，积极参加社区活动，与班上同学良好互动，非常快速地适应了不同文化的生活。第二个故事是关于越野比赛运动，从最初别人的不屑，到后来大家的尊重，说明了作者为此付出的努力。最后一个故事则是从苏州石桥得到的启示，最终设计出的模型桥获得班上的第一。
所有的这些故事都是以小看大。没有什么惊心动魄的大事或是感天动地的情怀。可是作者将它们娓娓道来，很好地支持了自己开头的表态：I am definitely among those that move。“我”属于第三类人！同学们在写作的时候可以借鉴这种写法，分段分故事来写，这样整个脉络就会清楚明了。
