三　Yale University 耶鲁大学
学生背景
Charles读的是加拿大高中，申请的是耶鲁的经济学专业。有人好奇是不是他做生意的爸爸从小就给他灌输做生意的想法。他回答说做生意和经济学是两回事。他也是学了经济学之后才发现生活变得不一样了。他就讲了一个故事，这就是他文书中最重要的部分。他特别喜欢打高尔夫球，而且在国外也是高尔夫球队长。但是回到北京以后就打不到高尔夫球了，他就和开高尔夫球的地方谈判，这个谈判过程是非常辛苦的。场地成本、器材费用都需要考虑。通过这件事情，他开始觉得经济学其实是活的。能看到这点非常不容易，这是个亮点。耶鲁这个学校是一个非常学术、培养精英的地方。其实，真正好的家教，并不是宠爱孩子，而是教会孩子运用成熟的理论，在生活中慢慢找到自己的方式。Charles恰恰具备了这一点，所以文书中选择了这件事情。（相关范文见案例一）
案例一
题目1
Why Yale
What in particular about Yale has influenced your decision to apply? Please limit your response to the space provided. (*Be sure to check your response in Print Preview to be sure that what you have typed displays properly.) Characters available 500.
是什么样的原因让你选择耶鲁？请在下面空白处作答（注意：请在打印预览中检查你的回答，确保显示无误）。500字符。
分析
500字符也就是80字左右，所以一个极佳的答案一定是非常具体的，聚焦于一个特点才能写出耶鲁的特殊之处。诸如“world-renowned faculty, sprawling campus, ambitious student body and rolling hills”（世界知名的教师，广阔的校园，雄心勃勃的学生和连绵起伏的丘陵）这样的文字就不要写了。
在所有的常春藤大学中，耶鲁的优势其实非常有限。哈佛和普林斯顿等学校同样有顶尖的教授和强势的人文学科，到底耶鲁是哪个方面吸引了你？
描述对你来说最感兴趣的一点。如果你想就读音乐系，你可以研究一下耶鲁新兴的面向本科生的歌剧公司；如果你想读书期间去欧洲做交换生，就查查他们的合作学校。对于我们中国学生来说，实地考察不太可能，但好在现在网络相当发达，去看看耶鲁的视频、页面，相信你的收获会非常大。
范文
Through family connections, my acquaintance with the JFP holdings under guidance of Mr. Perkowski was one of my most exciting personal memories. Jack, a prestigious Yale alumnus and the savoir of Paine Webber who raised 300 million of equity right after the "Black Monday”, mentors me rigorously and encourages me earnestly. He is in every way an elegant, erudite, sharp and liberal minded person, and exactly that kind of person I want to become after Yale.
分析
作者的回答独具匠心。开篇就提出由于家人的关系，结识到了一位著名的耶鲁校友Jack，并对债券有了一定的认识。作者还讲到这位校友的优雅、博学、敏锐以及开明这些优秀的品质，而这就是作者进耶鲁之后希望成为的那类人。一个简单却又有力的结尾！

题目3
Additional Essay
In this essay, please reflect on something you would like us to know about you that we might not learn from the rest of your application—or on something that you would like to say more about. We ask that you limit your essay to fewer than 500 words. Before you begin, we encourage you to go to http:// admissions.yale.edu/essay, where you will find helpful advice.
在这篇文章里，请写一些你想让我们了解的或是之前通用文章里写过，但你想多说些的。请把字数控制在500以内。
在动笔之前，我们建议你不妨去下面这个网站看看：http://admissions.yale.edu/essay，里面有不少有用的建议。
分析
这是一个非常好的机会，你可以向录取委员会的人展现你不同的一面。跟以前说过的一样，“真实”原则是重中之重。动笔之前，全面地看看你之前写过的通用文章。如果你之前选择的是一些沉重的话题，在这个地方最好就换换策略。尽管耶鲁挑选出来的人来自不同的国家，有着不同的兴趣爱好，但这些成功的申请人都有着极为相似的共同点：简洁精辟的写作、领导者的潜能以及个性独特的观点。
范文
作为一个经济学爱好者，我能将书本知识和生活结合起来。我认为这会帮助我成为一个对耶鲁有贡献的人。
As a student passionate about economics, I have the ability to integrate textual knowledge into life, which I believe will help me become a contributing member in the Yale community.
过去的经验中，我确实将经济学和金融所学运用到了现实生活。从学校到工作地，经济概念的使用无处不在。
In my past experiences, I have actually applied the textual knowledge of economics and finance in real life. From school to work place, economic ideas are used everywhere.
我将社会优化、免费商品和社会的外部性这些经济概念运用在谈判当中，成功将老板说服进而拿到高尔夫锦标赛资格以及高尔夫教学资格，从而实现了我的愿望，让高尔夫这项运动更加平民化。
When I was trying to help my golf team in Beijing to start our own golf tournaments, I utilized multiple economic theorems to enhance my chances convincing the golf clubs in Beijing. Golf is never considered a sport in China but rather, a division between the wealthy and poor, making golf a luxury. So, in hope of holding more tournaments at cheaper rate, I adapted multiple concepts of economics in my negotiation, like social optimization, complimentary goods and also social externality, aiming to strengthen my stand on hosting tournaments at a lower green fee. As a result, by utilizing my understanding of economic concepts, I was able to secure great deals and also contracts for golf courses to agree and sign with my team.
本段讲述了我实习的公司生产出的便携式音乐盒存在的问题，我通过市场调研、顾客使用反馈，大胆建议公司开发一套手机应用软件用以去控制产品。这样便将问题解决了。
When I was working for my father in the summer of 2011, I furthered the application of my textual knowledge. The company manufactured a portable music box that didn't have a speaker. It generated sound by vibrating the table with a metallic rod at bottom of box. It also had other features such as downloading music straight from the internet and also an alarm clock. When I interned there, I found the product less applicable and also not appealing to its market. The touch screen lacked control. The internet modem was too slow. The music box lacked a trade mark, in other words, a symbol that appealed to its market. I planned to create a revision plan that would include the details like the design of symbolic feature and experiment ways to lower costs. Through market research, and looking through feedbacks from costumers, I found the concept of comparative advantage applicable in this case: our company could also use something to its advantage. Since the product had to be bundled to a phone in order for the internet to work, I suggested that the company could set up a phone application to control the product from the phone. This solved two problems: the extra costs of creating controls and also the ability to sell it as a complimentary good for the phones. It was an idea that many people supported.
耶鲁的经济学项目世界闻名，我非常希望能进入耶鲁学习。我有着较多的国际经验，一定能为今后的耶鲁添砖加瓦。
I hope to become a member of the Yale University so I can become more capable in the fields of business and economics. Yale has one of the best economic programs with its Yale School of Management being the top of the world. I can also contribute to the community for my strong ability in innovation. Also as an open-minded individual, I have had extensive exposure to international experiences, which will surely adds flavour to the diverse community as Yale is.
分析
作者在首段开门见山指出自己对经济的喜爱以及运用书本知识的能力。接下来的两段便描述了二次运用经济学知识去解决问题的经历。最后一段陈述为什么选择耶鲁大学作为自己的梦想学校。
文章逻辑清楚，虽很少见到连接词，但信息一个接一个紧密合理。可以想见这是一个作文功底很高的选手。中间两段经历的讲述尤为出彩，从中我们可以看见这是个什么性格的女生——有自己的兴趣爱好，易于和人打交道，敢于提出质疑。通过这两段简洁精到的描述，让录取委员会了解自己是怎样的人。这就是作者的高明之处。
案例二
题目
If you selected one of the engineering majors, please write a brief third essay telling us what has led you to an interest in this field of study, what experiences (if any) you have had in engineering, and what it is about Yale's engineering program that appeals to you.
如果你选择了工程专业的其中一项，请写一篇文章简要说明为何对工程学习感兴趣，在该领域你有过的经历（如果有的话）以及耶鲁的工程项目哪方面吸引了你。
分析
“为什么感兴趣，有过的经历，该学校能给你带来什么”这三点大多数的申请者都能回答得很好。但请记住一点，不要让写出来的感觉像是机器人写的。带上些许激情和活跃。记住：Try to be unique!
大家可以对比这篇文章和前面哈佛大学工程类的文章，立意和风格就完全不同。
范文
我喜欢工程，从骨子里喜欢。毫不奇怪，这种对工程的喜爱源于对物理的狂热。接着讲述物理和工程之间的关系。
I love engineering. It is in my blood. Not surprisingly, my passion for engineering actually grows from my love of physics. Every twist and turn, every puzzle and solution in physics exert immense influence on me. Physics constitutes the very core elements and actions of our world, and engineering, building upon such constitution, transforms such elements and actions for our world. Physicists abstract the core elements and actions of our world, and engineers utilize and realize them.
讲述给女友买礼物的过程，机缘巧合让我意识到对工程的喜爱。
It was one improperly chosen gift to my girlfriend on her 17th birthday that allowed me to see more clearly my passion and dream for engineering. Seeking for a "perfect" gift for a whole week, I found it hard to pick one that could satisfy me—before I subconsciously chose a three-dimension, 500-piece jigsaw model of the Sailboat Hotel of Dubai. Every piece of the three-dimensional puzzle is made of synthetic material, and at first all the pieces are in the form of flat stacks, which means she needs to assemble the piece by piece orderly from the bottom up to complete the whole thing. Otherwise, they are all meaningless, lackluster small pieces in a gift box. Probably I forgot that I was sending the gift to a girl. When I got to the birthday party, my girlfriend was surprised to see the gift. "Oh my, are you buying the gift for yourself?" She was both amused and annoyed. "Go assembling it for me.”
这就是我：一个坐在那儿啃着比萨饼，在吵闹的音乐和混乱中疯狂地装配模型的人。我原先以为修建房子就是简单地将四面墙和屋顶用胶水粘连起来或是用钉子钉在一块，其实不然，这时我突然明白不同墙面的互相支撑意味着什么。这一刻我真真实实地触摸感受到它了。
That was me, sitting there alone, biting a piece of cheese pizza (I did not deserve a slice of cake at all), and crazily assembling the model among all the loud music and pandemonium. Was it some magic that seduced me to buy this huge junk? Why am I doing this in a great party? Maybe she was right; I probably picked a perfect gift for myself. And it all quieted down, as I started to feel the tempo of actions in my assembling work. I started to have a very unusual and keen feeling: before that, my understanding of a structure was very vague and I thought that a house was simply the combination of four walls and a roof, with some glues to patch them up, or with nails to bond them together. But when the model was completed in an hour, I examined my stunt and realized the meaning of mutual supports between different walls, the delicateness of different parts within a horizontal or vertical layer, the intended stability needed for a buttress, the flexible and rigid connections in every base between walls. Believe it or not, this was the awakening moment for me. Before that moment, I only "think" engineering, but now, I literally "touch and feel" it. I generated something new, not just reflecting on something. I made something useful, not just abstracting on something. And that really feels good.
介绍在耶鲁的暑期学习带给自己的收获。耶鲁授课老师有很多是著名的工程师，学校提供各种各样的全球活动，让学生有向不同学术领域的学生学习的机会。
After my girlfriend saw my "work" and hugged me with joy, I felt more convinced that engineering can be magic. And my summer studies in Yale further reinforced such a commitment in me. When I sat in some classes, I was surprised at the fact that most of undergrad classes are taught by renowned engineers—people who believe in their undergraduates and are dedicated to investing as much as in them as they do for postgraduates. This is why we enjoy the lectures, and we can choose our own curriculum as a complete individual with free action and free minds in Yale. And my eyes go beyond the classroom to see Yale's Global and Intercultural Study Initiative (GISI), a program to provide a wide variety of global engagement and learning opportunities to students with diverse academic interests at international and domestic sites. I can't wait to try many different programs inside or outside the engineering program, as such walls of encampment never exists here. I will also try Yale's Global Intercultural Experience for Undergrads and young engineers connected to the world programs. To see the world, to know yourself, to walk in and out of the books, isn't it a life that I dream to have for my college?
耶鲁能将我塑造成一名让改变发生的领导者。
For me, an easy choice indeed, Yale will help me evolve into a leader who can make changes happen.
分析
期望一位高中生对工程专业有特殊的感受是不可能的事。我想各位都有这样的体会，每天坐在教室里听老师讲数理化，怎么写出新意啊，没有什么可以写呢！苦苦冥想半天，半个字也憋不出来。
这篇文章的作者也是跟大家一样的高中生，而他的聪明之处在于很好地把工程和物理绑在了一起，告诉录取委员会的人为什么他对工程感兴趣，这点在文中的第一段就可以看到。而接下来的两段中，他则讲述了跟工程有关的一次非常有意思的经历——源于给女友买生日礼物。而正是这次经历让他真正知道了自己要什么和自己对工程的狂热之情。第四段在此基础上提出在耶鲁的学习经历，也正是它让作者坚定信念投身于工程专业学习。此外，作者提到耶鲁提供的诸如Yale's Global Intercultural Experience for Undergrads and young engineers connected to the world programs的项目让他非常感兴趣。最后一段提到选择耶鲁大学的原因——“让我的梦想成为现实”。纵观全文，很好地问答了题目中的三个问题，尤其是中间段的写作，描写细致生动，很不错！
