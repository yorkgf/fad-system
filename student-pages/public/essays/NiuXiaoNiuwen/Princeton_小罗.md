二　Princeton University 普林斯顿大学
学生背景
小罗来自广东，这位同学的特别之处在于她的经历和专业选择与众不同。首先，她的爸爸妈妈都是老师，本身对学术兴趣、专业爱好就很清楚，很早就定好了要学人类学和社会学。其次，她从事的活动也很突出。作为一名高中女生，她已经有过两次独自出国的经历。一次是去尼泊尔考察当地一种失传的语言，另一次是去非洲考察斑马的保护。她觉得人类学最有趣的地方就是田野调查。申请过程中她多次主动和教授联系，这个方法是我们以后应该提倡的，这叫作“套瓷”。当然，她还从事过其他一些有趣的活动，但是我觉得上面几点更能突出她未来作为一名人类学家的特点。这也非常符合普林斯顿这种学术性较强学校的特点。（相关范文见案例一）
案例一
题目
Essay: Your Voice
In addition to the essay you have written for the Common Application, please select one of the following themes and write an essay of about 500 words in response. Please do not repeat, in full or in part, the essay you wrote for the Common Application.
除了通用文章之外，请任意选择下列主题写一篇500字的文章。请不要重复你在通用申请里面的文章。
Please do not double space your essay.
请不要使用双倍字距。
◎选择1
Option 1　Tell us about a person who has influenced you in a significant way.
请向我们描述一位对你产生重大影响的人。
分析
这个题目跟通用申请里面的题目会有重合的地方，这就恰恰需要我们注意，额外的文章不允许我们的回答跟之前写过的重复。这就对我们提出了极高的要求。如果你之前的通用文章里面提到了这个问题，那么你最好换个话题来写。对于这个话题来说，要想写得好，不仅要告诉他们是什么人影响了你，还要向他们解释她或他是怎样影响你的。
需要注意的地方：
对于这个问题的回答常常是父亲和母亲。很多高中生爱写这个话题，笔者也感到很欣慰。但是如果你没有特别的故事，尽量还是别写他们。这么说并不是因为他们对你没有影响。事实上，对于我们大多数人而言，都是深受他们的影响。但是，这样写的人实在是太多了，你这篇文章投过去就像是投到茫茫大海当中，没有任何闪光点。如果你一定要写这个话题，那就要写出独特的地方来。比方说，特别引人注意的介绍，父母的昵称，父母的特殊事例。记住一点，事情本身描述得越细致，就越能出彩。多用一些例子，少用形容词。
◎选择2
Option 2　Using the statement below as a starting point, tell us about an event or experience that helped you define one of your values or changed how you approach the world.
请使用下面的句子作为开头，讲述曾帮助你定义自己的价值观或改变了你世界观的一个事件或一次经历。
"Princeton in the Nation's Service" was the title of a speech given by Woodrow Wilson on the 150th anniversary of the University. It became the unofficial Princeton motto and was expanded for the University's 250th anniversary to "Princeton in the nation's service and in the service of all nations.”
“在国家服务中的普林斯顿”是伍德罗·威尔逊在学校150周年的讲话标题。从那时起，它成为普林斯顿大学的非官方座右铭，并在学校250周年庆的时候扩展成了“在国家服务中和全世界服务中的普林斯顿”。
—Woodrow Wilson, Princeton Class of 1879, served on the faculty and was Princeton's president from 1902-1910.
——伍德罗·威尔逊，普林斯顿大学1879级学生，毕业后留校担任教师，1902—1910年任普林斯顿大学校长。
分析
选择1和2都强调了一点“tell us about an event or experience that helped you define one of your values or changed how you approach the world.”（讲述曾帮助你定义自己的价值观或改变了你世界观的一个事件或一次经历。）这两个问题其实都是让申请者去讲述一个故事。那么你应该做的就是挑选一个经历，大的也好小的也好，将它分享给录取委员会的人。一旦你开始讲述，请一定要强调它对你的影响。不同的事件不同的角度都可以被你拿来所用，从宏观的全球性活动到小的个人事例都可以。
◎选择3
Option 3　Using the quotation below as a starting point, reflect on the role that culture plays in your life.
请使用下面给出的引语作为开头，阐述文化在你生活中扮演的角色。
"Culture is what presents us with the kinds of valuable things that can fill a life. And insofar as we can recognize the value in those things and make them part of our lives, our lives are meaningful.”
文化给我们提供了各种有价值的东西用于填补生活，只要我们能认识那些东西的价值，并让他们融入我们的生活，我们的生活就会变得有意义。
—Gideon Rosen, Stuart Professor of Philosophy, chair of the Council of the Humanities and director of the Program in Humanistic Studies, Princeton University.
——吉迪恩·罗森，斯图尔特哲学教授，普林斯顿大学人文科学理事会主席和人文研究项目主任。
分析
拿到这个题目，不少人开始感叹——太难了！写什么呀？的确这道题不好写，难点在于如何去定义文化。问你一个问题，让你用中文说说文化是什么，你能解释得清吗？
据统计，有关“文化”的各种不同的定义至少有二百种。笼统地说，文化是一种社会现象，是人们长期创造形成的产物。同时又是一种历史现象，是社会历史的积淀物。确切地说，文化是指一个国家或民族的历史、地理、风土人情、传统习俗、生活方式、文学艺术、行为规范、思维方式、价值观念等。
所以，大胆写吧！只要是和以上我提到的相关，你都可以写。这样一看，也许这道题比其他几题都还好写呢。把你的思维打开，想想以上的任一点给你带来的启示，国内的国外的都可以。
◎选择4
Option 4　Using a favorite quotation from an essay or book you have read in the last three years as a starting point, tell us about an event or experience that helped you define one of your values or changed how you approach the world. Please write the quotation, title, and author at the beginning of your essay.
请使用一个你最近三年内在某篇文章或某书本上看到的句子作为开头，讲述曾帮助你定义自己的价值观或改变了你世界观的一个事件或一次经历。请将引语内容、文章或书本题目和作者写在文章开头。
分析
该题跟题目2差不多，唯一的区别是让你自引一个句子作为开头，题目2则是给出了一个句子。相对而言，这个题目更开放，只要是你感兴趣的都可以写。
范文（for Option 4）
20天的保护地球志愿者工作结束，我想自己的旅程算是到头了，但实际上却不是如此。该段为总结性开头，后文叙述来龙去脉。
Mahatma Gandi said, be the change that you want to be in the world. Watching the incessant flow of traffic and crowds, here I am, at a shabby corner somewhere in the downtown Nairobi. After twenty days for my Earthwatch conservation volunteer work for the endangered Grevy's zebra in Samburu, an extremely marginalized district in North Kenya, I thought my journey was over, but no, not now.
反思：花光自己所有钱，劝说父母支持自己远征肯尼亚，到最后就要被挫折击败吗？艰苦的努力就要付诸东流吗？
While waiting 24 hours for the Kenya Airways flight to finally arrive, why, I ask myself, I devoted all my pocket money and persuaded my parents to support my expedition in Kenya, only to find myself debilitated and disoriented by frustration. Is my painstaking effort totally futile?
回顾自己的旅行，我第一次意识到有些东西必须亲自经历才能领悟。
Looking back on my journey, I recall the wonderful tribe dance with Samburu warriors in the local village. When a tall, dark warrior with newly dyed red braids and colorful stripes on his face held my hand to dance, I suddenly felt an electric shock passing on to my beating heart. Through my left hand, I was touching the so-called savage, a purely natural world previously unknown to me. For the first time in my life did I come to realize that there's something I must experience in person to comprehend.
虽然语言不同、肤色不同，但我们共同分享喜悦和忧伤，期盼过上好的生活，也同时敬畏大自然的力量，所以说来自不同国家的人并不是孤立的个体，相反，我们是有着共同点的家人。
The power of link between us, who come from such distinct worlds, can virtually be stronger than the cultural differences. Despite our various tongues and skin colors, we all have our joys and sorrows; we share the same desire for better life; we all awe the power of nature. It was revealed to me that we are not isolated individuals but family sharing so much in common.
在这些天中我晒黑了皮肤，经历了干旱，与贫穷孩子为友，了解到不同种族间的冲突与矛盾，这些都让我慢慢读懂了一个真正的非洲，一个完全不同的世界。
Through these days I got tanned on my skin thanks to the parching sun; I witnessed how the drought impacted on the pastoral life; I made friends with children in rugs, I learned about the conflicts between different tribes and I struggled through airsickness and restless nights. If I have never touched their plight so keenly and closely, how can I come to understand the real Africa and a totally different life?
作者在这里表明希望具备新闻精神和人道主义，不断去探索、了解、报道进而去明白世界规律。
Now Jane Goodall's words eventually make sense to me.“Only if we understand can we care." I want to live a life as Jane, constantly exploring, understanding, reporting and knowing the world with a good journalism spirit and a humanitarian mind.
身在异国，受到质疑是很难免的。我学会了面对怀疑、冷漠甚至是敌意。我的声音就是我的命运。
"Chinese girl?" A halting voice suddenly interrupted my thought—a young black man examined me with his eyes curiously. True, it's my first time to feel like a stranger and outsider in such a remote country, but it will by no means be the last time. As I always long for amplifying those invisible people's voices and evoke my peers' attention to these people, who crave for understanding and aid from the outside world, I know I will confront doubts, aloofness, or even animosity. My voice is my fate.
我虽然依然孤身一人，但不再在市中心繁忙的交通和人群中等待，现在的我跳上一辆出租车，前往机场继续我的旅程。
Still all alone but no longer waiting in the busy downtown with unceasing stream of traffic and crowds, I hopped onto a taxi and headed to the airport continuing my journey.
分析
作者给我们讲述了她在非洲作为保护地球志愿者的经历，地点是在肯尼亚首都内罗毕。在旅途中，她观看了部落舞蹈，颜料染成的红色辫子，脸上色彩缤纷的条纹，这些都给了她无与伦比的感受。她跟当地人一起分享喜悦和忧伤，像一个大家庭一般。这些天，她经历了晒伤、干旱、无眠的夜晚，了解到了真正的非洲和真正不同的生活。
有了这次非洲之行，历经了肯尼亚的文化，作者在心里有了对自己的答案。紧接下来，作者引出一句话“Only if we understand can we care.”（唯有了解，我们才会关心。）她讲出了她的梦想，为生活在苦难中的人们去代言，去给他们争取更好的生活。具备新闻精神和人道主义，不断去探索、了解、报道进而去明白世界规律。
这篇文章的逻辑思维非常清楚，通篇没有一字讲到文化。但全篇读完，作者心目中的文化是什么，想必大家都能意会出来。而文化带给她的变化和启迪又是什么，正如最后一句话所说：“continuing my journey”。
案例二
题目
Engineering Essay工程类文章
If you are interested in pursuing a B.S.E. (Bachelor of Science in Engineering) degree, please write a 300-500 word essay describing why you are interested in studying engineering, any experiences in or exposure to engineering you have had, and how you think the programs in engineering offered at Princeton suit your particular interests.
如果你有兴趣就读B.S.E.（工程科学学士）学位，请写一篇300～500字的文章，说明你为什么对学习工程感兴趣，在工程方面你有过的经历或是接触以及普林斯顿大学这个项目怎样满足你的需求。
分析
这篇文章相对于那些模棱两可的题目来说，是再好写不过的了。题目内容明明白白地给了三个必答点：为什么感兴趣；有过的经历；该学校能给你带来什么。这篇文章没有什么创新点，直白地告诉录取委员会的人你的内心独白、你的真实想法，这就够了。
申请学校可以选择不同的专业，既然你选择了工程专业，那就要写出自己在这方面的兴趣和爱好。注意，哈佛大学的工程专业并不是很强。常春藤的学校大多有这样的特点。
范文
首段解释自己发明急救箱的想法以及具体的操作。
If I have a chance to invent something, I will invent some moveable or portable First Aid Kit, a convenient clutch set or splint that can easily fit and adjust to the wounds people suffer. The key is to have the right materials and proper human engineering for this First Aid Set. The materials, in this sense, should not only fit for the purpose of fastening or stabilizing the wound or fractured parts, but also congenial to human physical demands. It will not cause contagious, it is light and useable, and it is cheap and affordable as well.
交代自己产生发明急救箱想法的原因：热爱打篮球却因此受伤无法痊愈。
This idea actually comes from my own experiences. First of all, I am a boy of action who likes sports, especially the team sport or contact sport. Basketball is my favorite. It companied with me for a long time since I was in primary school. I learned the importance about cooperation with each other and the joy of work as a team. Sometimes, I was the team captain and I had to ensure that all my team members were in the most suitable position for them and made the power of 1+1 stronger than 2. It was a nature progress. Unfortunately, my knee was hurt in a basketball game by accident and never recovered again.
如果当时有合适的夹板或离合器，我现在本应该痊愈了，而现在我只能在一旁看别人打球。所以我想在大学学习材料科学与工程专业。以后在这个领域尽自己最大努力做出贡献，从而帮助他人。
At that time, if there were some suitable splint or clutch set for my injured leg, I should have recovered now. Afterwards, while sitting outside and watching my team members play basketball, I decided to do something for others to avoid the same tragedy. Were there some materials that could make up of better splint, my story would not have happened again. So I want to study more about Materials Science and engineering at college. One day in the future, I wish I can find the most suitable materials for all the medical appliances or quick sports injury relief. I know I cannot help anybody in the world, but, at least in the field of materials science, I will try my best to make my own marks in the field.
另一个原因则是在柬埔寨的经历让我了解到当地医疗的缺乏。当地的孩子只要一受伤，就几乎很难恢复了。所以我暗下决心，今后的我一定为他们提供帮助，尽我所能为他们创造一个更好的环境。
Moreover, this innovative idea also has roots in my experiences with the orphans during my volunteer actions, and also my long-held dream to build clutches for some of the orphans who suffered from landmines and afflicted injuries. I saw many kids in Cambodia or China who suffer a lot from lack of proper medical appliances. And the worse thing is, injured children are 52 times more likely to be injured in the same part of body by the same harm again than that of other kids. I know how important to the kids to have a safe and secure life. Most of us treat it normal that after class or work we can exercise and do some sports. But the orphans know it deeply, because once they were injured, they would hardly recover, making their lives even worse. For them, a protection or sense of security in playing areas is really necessary to them. Once, talking with the kids in my Cambodian trip, I asked them, "What is it you are so afraid of in playing with others?" They answered me, "Hurt." At that time, I choked down tears and promised to all of them, "You deserve anything you want. I will help you." So I really want to learn well about material science and engineering. One day in the future, I can deliver my promise to them, which is to not only make safe their playground, but also provide them with necessary aid or healing appliances, so that they may not be in harm's way again.
普林斯顿能让我通向成功，实现我的梦想。材料科学与工程专业则是我的理想。
I believe in Princeton, such a plan of learning will go smoothly and productively towards a destination of success. I also want to pursue an undergraduate degree in Materials Science and engineering right here in Princeton as well.
分析
该篇文章最大的亮点就是真实感人。通过作者叙述自己打篮球时的意外受伤以及在柬埔寨的亲身感受，我们能感受到作者希望发明便携式急救药箱的迫切愿望。也正是源于此念，作者非常希望在普林斯顿学习B.S.E.学位。
本文真实感人不仅是因为作者描述的是发生在自己身上和周围的事物，还在于作者高超的写作技巧。试想，同样的材料，你能写得让读者潸然泪下吗？试举一例：I asked them, "What is it you are so afraid of in playing with others?" They answered me,“Hurt." At that time, I choked down tears（哽咽着满眼是泪） and promised to all of them, "You deserve anything you want.”通过白描人物的对话，加强了文章的震撼力。而这是平铺直叙所达不到的效果。以后的写作中可以一试。
