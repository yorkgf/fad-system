二十　University of Michigan 密歇根大学
学生背景
密歇根大学是一所倡导独立自主、客观而又富于人文精神的大学。小廖同学自小就喜欢舞蹈、表演和钢琴，而且在这些方面均有很高造诣。父母建议她要么去读艺校，专门学习艺术课程；要么去读普通学校放弃艺术。因为他们认为如果二者兼修的话，肯定都是成绩平平，什么都学不好。小廖却有自己的想法，她认为一个人必须全面发展，否则将可能残缺不全。于是她毫不犹豫地选择了普通学校，努力学习文化课，而且一如既往地坚持练习舞蹈和表演、钢琴。小廖还是个非常独立的孩子，初中时就只身一人来北京读书，后来还辗转换了几个学校。生活中的她还非常热心公益，比如慰问远征军老兵等。小廖还喜欢根据网友的喜好写作，并将受欢迎的文章出版发行，而且联系出版商、编辑等出版事宜均是她独立完成。从小廖的人生经历中，她独立自主的能力和强烈的人文精神显而易见，所以密歇根大学录取她也是情理之中。
案例
题目1
Essays:
Everyone belongs to many different communities and/or groups defined by (among other things) shared geography, religion, ethnicity, income, cuisine, interest, race, ideology, or intellectual heritage. Choose one of the communities to which you belong, and describe that community and your place within it. (Approximately 250 words)
根据不同的地理、宗教、族群、收入、美食、兴趣、种族、意识形态或智力水平，每个人都属于不同的社群或是不同的团体。选择并描述你所在的一个社群，然后告诉我们你在里面所处的位置。（250字左右）
分析
在动笔写作之前，花一分钟时间来想想这个提示到底是什么意思。学校到底在问什么？然后再想想你希望录取委员会的人知道些什么。虽然这个提示非常宽泛，但你的故事仍然能着重于细节。比如说，你是学生会主席，你是辩论队的一员，你能修理自行车，你组织过大型的歌唱比赛等。
在所有的文章中，这篇文章需要有一个清楚明了的主题。看完你文章的人应该很快能明白：（1）发生了什么；（2）它为什么重要。描述这个社群的同时，更为重要的是让录取委员会的人知道你在其中的位置。这样一来，你便成功了一大半。剩余的，就看你讲故事的能力了。
范文
本文讲述了作者与其他文学爱好者一起创办校园杂志《萤火虫》的经过。该杂志旨在呼吁大家一起爱护环境。文章凸显了作者对文学的热爱及深厚的文学素养。
Fire Fly, a school magazine when I was in high school, gives an expression of my enthusiasm with literature and has been an inseparable part of my life. As I had firing interest in literature, I established the magazine with some other literature enthusiasts. Spending three years in the magazine, I have deep feelings in it for the efforts I have spent in it and the progress I have made there. Since I was excellent in words and sentences, I was elected as the editor. It's my responsibility to publish quarterly magazine every three month. As it was a great challenge at the very beginning, I completed it with excellent performance. In order to maintain the good quality of articles, I called for articles not only inside the association, but, more importantly, among all students through the school internet. Naturally, there were my articles in every periodical. Every time I wrote hard with all my thoughts in order the article is instructive, just as the late article I issued in the magazine, Bears in the South Pole Floating in Small Ice Cubes. Various themes in each periodical, the static theme of the magazine was that about environmental protection, for as an environmentalist, I want to appeal to more people to protect environment through the great influence of words. With the efforts and cooperation, the magazine was very popular on campus. We, the people living with passions for words and pens, should be a group of pride and honor.
分析
看完这篇文章，不知道各位能否看出作者的社群是什么。答案很简单，就是学校。就学校这个社群而言，作者在里面担任的角色很多。首先是学生，听课、回答、做作业；其次是一个杂志的主编，在业余时间培养自己的文学素养；再次则是作者，在这里写出各种各样的文章和报道。有了这样的经历与见识，学生结合自己的信仰、目标和决心以及对大学的向往。
可以说，这篇文章很好地回答了这个题目，巧妙地与自己的日常生活和学习联系在了一起。所以说，一篇文章不在于思想有多深刻，或者活动有多惊人，而在于突出主题，把你的事情用你自己的语言描述好了，这样你就成功了。
题目2
Describe the unique qualities that attract you to the specific undergraduate College or School (including preferred admission and dual degree programs) to which you are applying at the University of Michigan. How would that curriculum support your interests? (500 words maximum)
你申请的密歇根大学的某专业有什么独特的气质（包括预录取和双学位课程）？它的课程如何满足你的兴趣要求？（最多500字）
分析
回答这个问题远比你想象的简单。试想一下，你是否对某一项目感兴趣？你是否愿意跟某一著名教授合作？不要重复官网上的各类数据，诸如教授数量、众所瞩目的项目、难能可贵的研究机会。这些他们都已经知道。关键是要问问你自己。你好奇吗？有创造力吗？能创新吗？你喜欢挑战吗？你可以解决问题吗？
范文
This is definitely a story of love at first sight. The moment I walked on to the Ann Arbor campus for a visit, I knew U-M was my destination for the next four years in my life.
17年来，我一直生活在环境严重污染的北京，而初入安娜堡分校我就被它充满活力又宁静宜人的校园环境深深地吸引了。
"Awesome!" would be an understatement, even though I actually yelled that out in Ann Arbor. I have been living in Beijing, a heavily polluted city, for seventeen years, and I felt very privileged to see such vibrant, serene, scenery in Ann Arbor. The Ann Arbor campus looks unbelievably attractive. It would be so enjoyable to read books sitting on the grass and feel the tranquility that I have never enjoyed before.
环境只是安娜堡分校吸引我的一部分，更重要的是它的国际化、多元化的学术氛围。
Scenery usually just makes me happy, but my thrill comes more from the idea of studying in U-M with so many internationally minded and diverse fellow students. The highly internationalized student body and scholars from every corner of the world sets a perfect stage for my growth. Therefore, sharing a classroom with students from over 100 nations or cultures will allow me refreshing perspectives, unique subtlety and even particular know-how to work in various parts of the world in future.
本段点出了我对经济和数学的兴趣，希望进入安娜堡分校发展自己的兴趣。
We are living in a global village and no economics or mathematics student can succeed without being able to see beyond the border, the disciplines, or our area of expertise. I want to major in economics or mathematics, and attending U-M can give me first-rate eduction in the basics of economics and math additionally, allowing me to further access lectures, graduate courses and opportunities from great places like Ross School of Business. U-M has so many student practicum opportunities for undergrads, giving me the best platform to sharpen my skills and core values to help me join the world of great business minds. I am eager to study with like-minded economic and mathematic students who love the classroom, but also can't wait to get out and apply what they have learned.
安娜堡分校的本科生由很多有名的教授授课，教授们不仅相信自己的学生，还专心致力于教学，这一点让我很惊讶，也很喜欢。我喜欢自己选课，喜欢不受束缚的思想和行动自由。
When I sat in some classes, I was surprised at the fact that most of undergrad classes are taught by renowned professors—people who believe in their undergraduates and are dedicated to investing as much as in them as they do for postgraduates. This is why we enjoy the lectures, and we can choose our own curriculum as a complete individual with free action and free minds.
我喜欢安娜堡分校各式各样的国际化学术活动，我已经迫不及待地去做各种尝试。这不正是我梦想中的大学生活吗？！
My eyes go beyond the classroom to see U-M's Center for Global and Intercultural Study (CGIS), a program to provide a wide variety of global engagement and learning opportunities to students with diverse academic interests at international and domestic sites. I can't wait to try many different programs, Michigan Global Academic Programs, Global Intercultural Experience for Undergrads, Spring/Summer Language Study, and Global Course Connections. To see the world, to know yourself, to walk in and out of the books, isn't it a life that I dream to have for my college?
For me, an easy choice indeed, U-M will help me evolve into a leader who can make changes happen. (495 words)
分析
你对密歇根大学了解什么？你愿意加入University Research Opportunity Program（大学研究机会计划项目）吗？你想去Biological Station（生物站）吗？你对the Institute for Social Research（社会研究所）感兴趣吗？这样的问题其实是比较难回答的，因为每个学校都问这个问题，大家写的时候也很艰难，那么怎么写出新意来呢？这个文章就是一个小小的范本，这里，小廖选择几个点和事用以说明自己的品质，然后把这个品质与密歇根大学的气质联系在一起，这样就可以了。
