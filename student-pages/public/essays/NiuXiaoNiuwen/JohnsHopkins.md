十三　Johns Hopkins University 约翰·霍普金斯大学
学生背景
小金同学来自北京，母亲在后海开酒吧，她从小就在酒吧目睹了母亲生活的艰辛。虽然如此，母亲却很鼓励她做任何自己想做的事情。印象最深的就是有一次她居然把自己喜欢的毛毛虫装进瓶子里带回了家，把平日里最怕昆虫的妈妈吓了一跳，但是妈妈并没有因此而命令她扔掉这些吓人的玩意儿。此外，小金同学从小就很喜欢钢琴，而且弹得非常好。妈妈建议她去音乐学院专门学习钢琴，但是她却拒绝了。因为她认为一所完整的人应该是一个全面发展的人，于是她选择了一所普通中学，选择的专业也是理工科的专业。同时，小金并没有就此放弃自己对钢琴的热爱，而是在学习之余依旧坚持每天练习弹钢琴。她还曾和自己的合唱队一起代表学校去莫斯科和斯德哥尔摩参加演唱，这两次特殊的经历使她对音乐有了更加深入的理解，也让她更加执著地热爱音乐、追求自己音乐梦想。
案例
Essays and Short Answer Questions Freshmen Applicants 新生必写
Students applying as freshmen to Johns Hopkins using the Common Application plus the Johns Hopkins Supplement must submit both the Common Application essay and both of the short answer questions below (250 word maximum each). Include your name, birth date, and the last four digits of your Social Security number on these and all other additional sheets.
约翰·霍普金斯大学申请者除提交通用文章外，还需提交简短的额外问答的文章（每篇最多250字）。在每篇页面内都请填写您的姓名、出生日期以及社会保险号码的最后四位。
题目1
Write a brief essay (250 words maximum each question) in which you respond to the following questions. (freshman applicants only)
请简短回答以下问题（每题最多250字）：
Johns Hopkins offers 50 majors across the schools of Arts and Sciences and Engineering. On this application, we ask you to identify one or two that you might like to pursue here. Why did you choose the way you did? If you are undecided, why didn't you choose? (If any past courses or academic experiences influenced your decision, you may include them in your essay.)
约翰·霍普金斯大学文理和工程学院提供超过50个专业的课程。在本次申请中，我们要求申请者确定一个或两个专业，并言明为什么做这样的选择。如果你还没有决定，言明为什么没有决定。（如果有任何学过的课程或学术经历影响到你的决定，可以包含到你的文章中。）
分析
题目要求申请者选择一个或两个将来学习的专业领域，并言明自己的理由，如果不选，也请言明自己的理由。这类问题的回答并不要求申请者对某一个问题进行长篇大论，而是落到某一点上，说明你的理由。无论你选择什么专业或者不选，都要给出自己的理由。记住，一定要简洁明了地表明自己的理由，字数不超过250个。
范文
言明自己选择的专业，并表达了自己对所选专业的激情与热爱。
I choose to apply for chemistry or applied science major in JHU. Ever since I first put my fingers on the test tubes in my chemistry course, I know that my life will be intertwined with it. A burning passion for elements and interactions of various forces, a pair of keen eyes and persistent love for it enable me to have a grander stage to play on in JHU, where I want to continue my passion in this field under guidance of great professors and like minded students. I also want to work as a lab assistant so that I can further practice and heighten my skills.
言明约翰·霍普金斯大学吸引自己的地方，如名牌的教授、教授对教学的热爱、实验室以及教学方法等。
JHU has more than one attraction to me. So many Professors with stellar records in science will coach me closely, and they love to teach undergraduate students because they value college level instructions the same as their research. I also like JHU's long time commitment to place the lab and practical skills as a center piece in its science education, training generations of students to be more fully enlightened in the class and beyond. Its broad range of exchange programs, foreign scholar seminars and lectures of the science frontline development are also favorites to a girl like me who loves to be challenged.
简单总结并再次表达自己对在约翰·霍普金斯大学学习的渴望。
I have dreamed of a place for me to develop the whole personality and excellent science expertise. There is simply no better place than JHU for me to learn, grow and play as a young scientist.
分析
正如前文对题目分析的那样，作者的文章并没有什么大开大合的转折与惊奇，也没有华丽的辞藻修饰。只是按照题目的要求，朴实无华地写出了自己的选择和理由，而这正是校方想要的东西。
题目2
Tell us something about yourself or your interests that we wouldn't learn by looking at the rest of your application materials. (While you should still pay attention to sentence structure and grammar, your response is meant as a way for us to get to know you, rather than a formal essay.)
告诉我们一些在你的其他材料中没有体现的关于你自己或自己感兴趣的东西。（请注意你的句式结构和语法，你的回答也是我们了解你的途径之一，即使不是正式的文章。）
分析
本题要求申请者讲述一些关于自己或自己感兴趣而又没有在其他申请材料中得到体现的内容，可以是任何东西。这是什么意思呢？其实就是校方希望通过的你的讲述，更多地了解你。正如后边括号中说明的那样，虽然不是正式的文章，但请注意你的表述方式，这也是他们希望了解你的一部分。所以，回答这个题目，务必注意两个方面，一是讲述内容的选择，二是表达方式要恰当。内容上应当选取一些比较特殊的，能展现你另一面的东西；表达方式上要充分得体。
范文
开篇说明，我的两场公共即兴合唱彻底改变了自己对音乐的观念。
It was the two impromptu chorus performances in public that changed utterly my perception of music.
讲述自己和合唱队其他成员在莫斯科地铁偶遇四位当地艺术家表演弦乐四重奏，并与之合奏齐鸣的经历。
As head of the school chorus, I led the team for a tour performance to Russia and Northern Europe. In a day, I along with several chorus members saw four artists playing string quartet in the walkway of Moscow subway. Mesmerized and overwhelmed by the music, I called on to my chorus members to assemble in silence, to get ready for the start of next score. We sang on the starting note of their next piece, in a perfect match of tones and rhythm, and passion for music. When our singing concluded at the end note of their playing, we continued, without any coordination at all, to sing and play the next piece. After a wonderful ensemble, four artists and us embraced each other. Although our languages were different, we talked and enjoyed music with the same passionate ways.
讲述了合唱队在斯德哥尔摩广场的一次成功的演唱经历。从这两次演奏中，我深刻地感受到了音乐力量的强大，加深了自己对音乐的理解。
Later, we travelled to Stockholm Municipal Hall for a performance. Upon waiting to enter the stage, I saw the crowds of visitors and tourists on the plaza, many of whom were not invited to our performance. All of sudden, I thought of that wonderful ensemble in Moscow subway. Get in a stage formation and ready your voices! I led the chorus to sing right away. More and more people from the plaza gathered around us and our singing bounced with more power and liveliness. Many audiences were so enchanted by our performances that they applauded loudly for an encore. I, as the conductor and leader, had the rare honor to be asked for some autographs!
分析
本文作者从自己的一份独特经历出发，真实地表现了自己不同寻常的一面，其表达方式合理，完全符合题目的要求，是一篇佳作。
题目3
Transfer Applicants 转学生必写
Students applying as transfers to Johns Hopkins are required to answer both the Common Application personal essay and the question below. Include your name, birth date, and the last four digits of your Social Security number on these and all other additional sheets.
申请转学到约翰·霍普金斯大学的同学除提交普通申请的文章外，还需回答如下问题。所有表格和其他所有材料都要包含你的姓名、生日和社会保险号的后四位。
In your personal statement on the Common Application, you explained why you are seeking to transfer. Please explain in detail why you wish to transfer to Johns Hopkins University specifically.（500）
在普通申请的个人陈述部分，你已经说明为什么要转学。现在，请详细说明为什么想转学到约翰·霍普金斯大学。（最多500字）
分析
这个题目是专门针对需要转学到约翰·霍普金斯大学的学生，除了要求递交和其他申请人同样的材料外，还须回答问题。另外在个人陈述部分要说明自己转学的原因。注意是详细的原因，切记不要泛泛而谈，尽说些空洞无用的东西。
