五　Stanford University 斯坦福大学
学生背景
小丁同学来自山东，各项素质都比较全面。他喜欢骑自行车、拉二胡，在美国交流的时候还做过志愿者，这充分说明他具备动手实践的意识和能力。另外，这个孩子的分数也很高，SAT2380，托福满分。斯坦福大学的特点是创新、革命和精英，所以文书中应该突出自己这方面的才能和想法。才能的突出不一定就是要做出与众不同的事情，把自己的想法做出来，付诸实践也是可以的。小丁同学有三个方面可以写进文书：第一，自行车一直伴随着他，他曾经从山东骑到北京，从北京骑到了兰州。第二，他非常喜欢戏剧和历史，甚至自己改编过戏曲，然后翻译成英文。第三，喜欢研究烹饪，自创菜品。所以文书方面应尽量将各方面都强调出来，使个人形象丰满起来。

题目7
Stanford students possess an intellectual vitality. Reflect on an idea or experience that has been important to your intellectual development.
斯坦福大学的学生有着良好的学术活力。请回想对你智力发展起着重要作用的一个想法或一次经历。
分析
关于这个题目，有些话也许有些人不爱听。这个题目其实是含蓄地告诉我们，如果你智力没那么高，在斯坦福这样的大学估计不能很好地融入进去，那最好就别申请了。其实进入斯坦福的学生没有在这个问题上有过犹豫，他们不会眼巴巴地望着题目束手无策。相反，在这个问题上他们都有着自己感兴趣的领域话题来回答这个问题。而这些话题都足以显示出他们在学术方面的好奇心或是对学术的热爱。最烂的回答莫过于此类：“I like math because there's always a right answer”或者是“History is so interesting, which is why I watch the History Channel.”虽然这么说很直白，但确实都是真话。
事实上那些取得高分的孩子无疑都是聪明的，但并不是所有这些孩子都是知识分子，即“intellectuals”。所谓的知识分子是那些喜欢学习的人，他们对事物有着高度的好奇心。他们往往不是被比赛去激发取得高分，更多的是源于对事物本身的好奇和对学习的兴趣。这些“贪婪”的学生就是斯坦福大学想要的。
所以，针对这个提示，在写的时候要想想到底是哪个科目吸引你，哪些东西能让你每天魂牵梦萦，又是哪些东西能让你有跟别人分享的欲望。需要提醒的是，这个写作的话题不仅局限于纯粹的学术，诸如数学一类。它也可以是电动车背后的工程理论，或是使用了最新技术的电吉他；它可以是在课余时间你在电脑方面的心得，或是跟同伴在读书会上分享英语学习的乐趣。总的说来，文章的主题应该反映出你对学术方面的好奇，对实现它的渴望以及在其过程中你付出的努力。
范文
此段中作者提到在商业评论上看到的3D互联网电视平台，介绍自己对这一技术的了解——它并不是仅仅提供上网冲浪，网上购物或三维图像的终端，它的独特性在于提供及时信息、信息反馈或搜索机制，并以云计算作为技术基础。
The most amazing idea I have came across so far was the 3D internet television platform. I read about this early this year in a Harvard Business Review article. Such a platform is not simply a terminal for internet surfing, online shopping or 3 Dimension images. The uniqueness of this platform is to provide an instant messaging, information feeding or searching mechanism with cloud computing as technical basis. I could not fully grasp the essences of cloud computing, but I can see the values of such great innovation. Just imagine you are watching "gossip girls" at home and want to chat with your friends in Seattle, you can just type in the messages that will appear on the screen and become viewable by them on the other side of the globe. More importantly, search, locate and find anything you want from these messaging and go anywhere from these "cloud" of data. Say your friend mentions to you in the messaging "Tina," you can find that info link and get everything needed for the topic of Tina.
我对这项技术所体现的大胆思维感到震惊，并相信它有着无限的可能性。通过阅读，一些我原有的概念和想法被完全颠覆了。从那时开始我学会了从多方面观察事物和保持一个更开放的心态。
I was struck by the sheer magic and audacity of thinking embodied in this technique. And my thoughts went beyond the screen of a single TV set. Can we do this on a wrist watch with the same features? Can we do the same on my I-phone? Or on my microwave stalled in the kitchen? Sure we can. I believe the magic lies in the endless possibility that we can do with this technique. We usually believe that internet technology can only be applicable on certain platforms or with some specific forms, but this innovation totally change my mind-set. I was kept awake all night after I read the article, overturning many ideas or concepts I used to hold as unchangeable, and extending my antenna of thinking to so many other areas unthinkable before. From then on, I look at many things around me with a more scrutinizing perspective and a more lively mindset. Just asking yourself several more "why not" than before could actually bring you surprises.
分析
文章讲的是在《哈佛商业评论》上看到的3D互联网电视平台带给自己的启发。正如上文的题目分析讲的一样，回答好这个问题不局限于纯粹的学术领域，只要是能给自己在智力发展方面有过促进作用的都可以写，此文就是一个极好的例子。
文章一共两个段落，第一段作者介绍了什么是3D互联网电视平台，“不仅提供上网冲浪，网上购物和三维图像，还提供及时信息和搜索功能”，并举绯闻女孩为例，说明这个平台的作用。第二段作者则讲到自己对这项高科技的惊讶。它给予了事物无限的可能性。作者此后学会了从各个角度认真细致地看待事物，并且明白了不要把思维局限在一个点上，而是要brainstorm，不断扩大思维广度。
题目8
Virtually all of Stanford's undergraduates live on campus. Write a note to your future roommate that reveals something about you or that will help your roommate─and us─know you better.
几乎所有的斯坦福学生都住在学校里面。请写一张说明你自己的小条给你的室友，让你的室友，也让我们，更加了解你。
分析
首先要说的是，诚实最重要。如果你真的要把这张小条给你未来的室友，你会去告诉他你在学业上多么刻苦，你如何成为奥运会志愿者，你多么渴望得到优秀毕业生的称号吗？
答案肯定是NO!对于这个问题的回答，你可以说说你对小提琴的热爱、对篮球的入迷，可以讲讲你去年参加的学校唱歌比赛，可以提提你对辣椒的喜爱——即使对你嗓子有伤害，但仍然挡不住你每天三顿辣的习惯。
做真我，实话实说，That's it! 这样就行了！
范文
My dear roommate in Stanford,
Here is a greeting from Ding Hao, your future companion in study and life at this great campus. I am an international student from China. There are so many exciting things we can do together on campus but here I am going to tell you just a few. First of all, I love bicycling, so if you are up for a joy ride or race, let us get it on as soon as we settle down in the dorm. I started to practice this since Grade 4 so let me warn you of my expertise and skill! Also, I love to read history and drama, many books of which subjects have accompanied me all the way from China to here. Want to take a look? You are welcome! I can also give you a hand-written bibliography of all my favorite books here, ranging from the Fall of Roman Empire, American Democracy to Modern Drama Series. I know we have drama clubs here in Stanford, and next time, the tickets to the first show are on me. I hate to play winning historical figures, yes, I did play in the high school drama before, but with a not-so-stellar record. Those figures or roles in tragedy, especially Greek tragedy or Japanese Samurai Time, can best convey the so-called hidden humanity aspects of us. We can discuss more on these roles indeed. And, I am kind of thinking starting a fraternity for gourmets. Food here, I need to say, is fairly good, but not diversified enough. If you are fond of edibles besides pizza slides, cram chowder soup or salted bread, we can do our own kitchen. I love to cook and weird as it may seem, I love to try all sorts of materials in cooking with totally revolutionary styles! Who says French cooking can not go along with the Chinese spices? Let us try some and test them on our friends, just making sure they are not suffering food poisoning. Now, let us do something together!
分析
作者在文中提到了三个兴趣点。第一是骑自行车，第二是读戏剧和历史方面的书，最后一个是吃美食。值得一提的是，全文俏皮可爱，清楚地展现了作者的幽默形象，比如“let me warn you of my expertise and skill!”，“Want to take a look?”，“the tickets to the first show are on me”，“test them on our friends”，“making sure they are not suffering food poisoning”。本来是一篇提交给招生委员会的假想书信，作者却写出了真实感，让人读来好似对面一般。可以说，这篇文章是成功的，一方面流畅俏皮，另一方面也让委员会的人看到了作者丰富多彩的业余活动和富有创见的思想。
题目9
Why do you choose Stanford?
你为什么选择斯坦福？
分析
这个问题几乎所有的学校都会问到。而要回答好这个问题，以下的两点错误万不能犯。第一，列举出一系列的常用优点，诸如You have a strong reputation, a beautiful campus and top professors等。这些在各个学校都能用到。第二，从斯坦福官方网站上复制粘贴一些句子，然后用在你的回答上。
“你为什么选择斯坦福？”要想回答好这个问题，不是去问斯坦福，而是要问问你自己。写作前，请详细周到地从网上搜索一些跟这所学校有关的、你感兴趣的领域，看看他们在该领域能够给你提供什么。此外，你可以想象你正身处这所学校中，图面中的你正在干什么？你对这所学校有什么期待？当你去学习的时候，你希望有怎样的改变？你对自己的学院感兴趣吗？学院的哪部分让你迫切希望进入学习？
所有的这些答案跟你之前的网上搜索结果是否一致？有了这个，你的作答内容自然是多了许多。
范文
我对人类学非常感兴趣。刚看到斯坦福，我就被它深深吸引。斯坦福悠久的历史最为吸引我。
With my great passion for Anthropology, I exerted every effort to select the most desirable university to pursue my dream. The first few moments I noticed Stanford, I was deeply attracted by it, which is definitely a love at first sight. To be honest, Stanford's long history is extremely fascinating to me.
斯坦福的教育排名前列，能让我接受良好的教育。我很希望跟着渊博的教授学习知识，我时常想象在斯坦福学习的画面。
I was amazed that Stanford has been a leader in higher education in the States and around the world for more than a century. As the fifth oldest institution of higher learning in the United States, it stands proudly as one of the true liberal science studies leaders. I admire such a development and accumulation, which, I believe, will ensure me very advanced education. And I am quite eager to study with the renowned and knowledgeable professors in Stanford, including those who have won Nobel Prize. Sometimes, I couldn't help drawing up the colorful picture for my future study in Stanford.
斯坦福校友Woulk说过一句话：上学期间最好的时刻是在斯坦福校园外；而人类历史和思想是在斯坦福校园内。斯坦福有其独有的魅力，以至于让我对其心驰神往。此外，斯坦福有着超过1000万册的藏书量，超过10万现刊和连载，大量的电子资源、手稿和稀缺图书。
Still vivid in my memory are those words, describing Stanford by its College alumnus HBS. Woulk "doubled magic" where "the best things of the moment were outside the rectangle of Stanford; the best things of all human history and thought were inside the rectangle." Though I couldn't totally understand the profoundness of these words, their magic power seemed to have controlled me, which strengthened my mind to explore more in Stanford so as to take the fullest advantage of my vast potential and intelligence. I was also delighted to find its wonderful libraries with over 10 million volumes, over 100,000 current journals and serials, and an extensive collection of electronic resources, manuscripts, rare books, etc.
我相信以我的努力和优秀，终有一天会实现进入斯坦福的梦想。
I believe a university experience at Stanford will be rewarding and invaluable. Soon enough, I dream that I learn with my professors and classmates in Stanford. And I believe with my great efforts and excellence, this day will come.
分析
这篇文章从四个方面来叙述想进斯坦福的原因。历史悠久、排名靠前、博学老师和丰富的藏书量。说实话，这些都是一些大白话，文章其实并没有给予我们具体而实在的原因。但难能可贵的是作者的作文功底。比如像“magic power seemed to have controlled me”,“strengthened my mind to explore more”,“couldn't help drawing up the colorful picture for my future study”等。
