十二　Cornell University 康奈尔大学
学生背景
小聂是北京一所国际学校的学生，喜欢文科专业，如社会学、国际关系学。她特别注重环保，而且还将自己的环保理念付诸实践，大学时曾举办过“地球一小时”环保宣传活动。更重要的是，她曾经跟爸爸一起开办过一个有机农场，还亲自参与了农场的选址、选择作物品种、管理、商业合作等一系列经营事项。虽然由于被骗，农场经营失败了，但这样一次经历对于她来说无疑是人生中的一笔宝贵财富。作为一名女生，能参与这样的项目也是难能可贵的。除此之外，小聂同学还很有爱心，她曾经去参观孤儿院，这次经历深深地震撼了她的内心，为此她开办了自己的英文报纸，吸引社会关注孤儿、关注慈善。就是这样一个学以致用、脚踏实地的孩子，用自己独一无二的经历和性格特质，让康奈尔相信自己就是学校一直寻寻觅觅、回首阑珊的人。注意，这个学校可以选择Alternate学院，即选一个主申请学院的同时还可以选一个备选学院，所以，这里收录了小聂的两篇文书。
案例
题目1
College of Agriculture and Life Sciences: How have your interests and related experiences influenced the major you have selected in the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences?
农业与生命科学学院题目：你的兴趣和相关的经历如何影响你在农业与生命科学院的专业选择？
分析
农业与生命科学学院是康奈尔的主要学院之一。分析此题目可知，他们对申请者的兴趣和相关经历相当重视。所以在回答此问题的时候，务必要注意将自己的专业选择与自身的兴趣和经历相结合。
范文
开篇言明康奈尔是自己理想的大学，进而从康奈尔的学生、教职员工和科学研究等方面赞美了康奈尔对纽约州、美国乃至世界的贡献，最后阐明康奈尔的专业特色与自己的职业规划非常契合。
Cornell is the ideal school for me to finish my undergraduate study. Faculty, students, and staff of Cornell discover, integrate, disseminate, and apply knowledge about food and energy systems, the life sciences, the environmental sciences, and economic and community vitality as a basis for sustainable improvement in the lives of people throughout New York, the nation, and the world. I can say Cornell's focus in teaching and training fit me of my career plan. From the university's only accredited undergraduate business program to innovative research in communication, development sociology, landscape architecture, the biological sciences, and other fields, CALS offers a broad spectrum of programs that extend past the traditional definition of an agricultural school. That idea exactly accords me with the understanding of what an agricultural school should be.
本段讲述了自己与父亲一起创办有机农场的特殊经历。在那段时间，父亲一直在阅读《世界有机农场组织》一书并告诉我这是我们有机农场的未来。他甚至还计划去美国参观。但是由于时间有限，没能成功。父亲希望我能帮助他完成这个梦想。
Years ago, my father was inspired by his friends to build our family organic farm and he invited me to assist him in various matters such as site selection, contract signing and other related matters. Five years of consistent hard-work along with our organic farm has made me turn from a junior high school boy into an ambitious and mature young man. I can never forget that during that time, my father always read a book called World Wide Opportunities on Organic Farms, and he kept saying to me, this is the future of our organic farm. And he even plan to go to the US to visit and inspect the more advanced farms, however, since time is limited, we didn't make that trip, but he sincerely wish I could help him finish that dream: go to the best agriculture school and visit the true organic farms. All in all I shall bear my father's teaching on how many roads must a man walk down before he becomes a man, and study hard no matter how hard it will be.
本段首先表明了自己对农业科技的浓厚兴趣，进而从自身对农业科学的了解、激情和愿望中再次表明自己的兴趣和经历与康奈尔农业与生命科学院是天作之合，毋庸置疑。
While enjoying the back-to-nature experience on our farm, and although the study of agriculture more or less has something to do with my father, I myself now, really become interested in the agriculture, in how to manage it well and in what to expect. What is more, I am now more aware of the importance of knowledge. Though it is just a small farm, in order to protect it well, business, economics, real estate, environmental protection, biological knowledge and pest inspection and prevention are all elements that need to be considered seriously and fully. Delightedly, after these unforgettable experiences, I have become more and more passionate for biology and agriculture, and also for other subjects in science, and a picture to be devoted to it to become a real professor has been clearer and clearer in my mind. Cornell with the best facilities in teaching and the most advanced technology around the world will definitely be the place for me to learn to fulfill my dreams. I apply to be a member of Cornell, I believe we are the perfect match.
分析
本文其实与其他很多申请者的文章并没有太大的区别，就是针对问题本身，直接而准确地作答。然而，其特殊之处在于作者本身经历的特殊性。作者在过去的几年中，与父亲一起创办自家的有机农场。经过选址、管理以及商业合作等一系列实践活动后，作者对现代农业有了自己的深刻体会，同时很好地说明了自己对农业科学兴趣的产生、发展，以致最终决定学习农业科学的过程。这样给人自然而然、水到渠成的感觉。
当然，不是所有的申请者都有幸有这样的经历，但每个人的人生经历都是无法复制且独一无二的，你要做的就是用自己的人生经历和兴趣来诉说，来说服校方，让他们信服，你就是那位他们寻寻觅觅、回首阑珊的人。
题目2
College of Arts and Sciences: Describe your intellectual interests, their evolution, and what makes them exciting to you. Tell us how you will utilize the academic programs in the College of Arts and Sciences to further explore your interests, intended major, or field of study.
文理学院题目：请描述你的学术兴趣、原因以及发展。此外，请告诉我们你将如何利用康纳尔的学术项目去进一步探索你的兴趣或研究领域。
分析
文理学院的文章其实和上文农业与生命科学学院的题目并没有太大的差别。写这样的文章并不难。题目明确告诉你三个必答点：对什么感兴趣，感兴趣的原因以及兴趣发展的过程；有过的经历；该学校能给你带来什么。针对这样的题目，申请者要做真实的自我，表达自己内心的感受，直白地告诉录取委员会的人你的内心独白、你的真实想法，这样就够了。
范文
开篇表达，我选择新闻学这个专业并不是自己一时兴起，而是经过深思熟虑的。
My choice to major in journalism comes a long way, for which I realized it not by sudden whims, but by in-depth and whole-hearted exploration of activities and meditation.
记叙自己参观当地孤儿院的经历和感想，孤儿院简陋的设施和孤儿天真无邪的笑容震撼了我的内心，我觉得应该做些什么，使这个人群受到社会的关注，只有这样，才有更多的人帮助他们。
In 10th grade I once organized a visit of our class to the local charity orphanage. The shabby houses and facilities in the orphanage, all in ironic contrast to the innocent smiles of the children, truly left me dumbfounded. Never had I faced such a revelation to their dilemma so keenly, that I decided to evoke people's attention to these ignored but vulnerable groups, for I believed aids would happen when people knew who need help.
进一步传播人道主义和人性关怀，我办起了自己的英文报纸Scope。
That's why I started to commit myself to some journalism works. With one hundred percent enthusiasm I founded Scope, our school's first English newspaper, in hope of conveying the mind of humanitarian and care. Journalism, in my eyes, is the seeking of truth and justice, conscience and redemption, a challenging and honorable pursuit. For a time, my Scope became my lover—I am seriously (too seriously!) convinced I would become a journalist one day.
随着Scope的发展，作者开始反思，他认为Scope需要更深层次的东西——对于我们想要去帮助的人，对其有这全面的理解和认识是必不可少的。
Through the effort of the whole crew, our Scope gradually earned recognition and audience. I once again reflected, however, that I need something more profound, since the words could only provide a limited and superficial touch to a totally different, alienated minority group. People may know who need help, but not how. It occurred to me that, without a holistic understanding of the people we are to help, disaster ensues instead of any assistance.
我的非洲之行，使自己第一次真正领略了人文研究者的工作、思维与风采，更加坚定了自己内心的想法。
In full ambition to learn the real Africa I joined the EW volunteer expedition in Guatemala. Luckily, I met Karla, our facilitator, who is an anthropologist focus on human conflicts. Under her influence I tried to perceive the world in an amateur journalist's eyes for the first time, to observe, feel and reflect the Native people in flesh and soul. From then on I discover a brand new possibility to help, through the learning in college, to reap a trained, broad-minded and scientifically rationalized comprehension to the world and the people. Anthropology means loves, cares, understanding and expertise in knowing our world. I find it deeply relates to my desire and passion.
最后，提出了自己想要加入康奈尔的想法，并心仪由大卫·霍姆伯格和凯瑟琳·马奇教授主持的尼泊尔研究项目。除此之外，作者还打算设计自己的课程——人类学与新闻专业的结合。
If I am lucky enough to be in the embrace of the College of Arts and Science in Cornell, where sharp-edged thoughts thrive freely, I would focus on the humanities, media studies and anthropology studies, in which my ambition centers, while maximizing my scope of study from ecology and biology, to government and economics, to acquire a holistic perspective of the world. If I can, I am hoping to join the Cornell/Nepal Study Program led by professor David Holmberg and Kathryn March, to touch the real Nepali culture. What's more, I intend to design my own combining major, like anthropology with journalism, which both suits my passions and adjusts to the uniqueness of Cornell's curriculum.
分析
这篇文章的亮点就在于真实感人，作者细致入微地描写了自己内心的逐渐转变，清楚明白地写出了自己的兴趣所在，兴趣是如何产生的，产生后又是如何一步步发展的。直到最后，想要通过在康奈尔的学习，进一步实现自己的梦想，丰富自己的人生。其实很多题目并不需要太多华丽的修饰，你只需要按照题目的要求，写出自己的真实状况即可。
另外，这个同学的英文水平确实不错，用词造句都有自己的风格，这都是平时学习积累的结果，非一日之功。
题目3
College of Engineering: Tell us about an engineering idea you have, or about your interest in engineering. Describe how your ideas and interests may be realized by—and linked to—specific resources within the College of Engineering. Finally, explain what a Cornell Engineering education will enable you to accomplish.
工程专业题目：请告诉我们你在工程方面的想法或兴趣并将它与康纳尔的研究相联系。最后请解释康奈尔对你的意义何在。
分析
工程学院的题目要求申请者提出一个有关工程的想法或自己在这方面的兴趣，并将它与康奈尔的研究相联系。这里值得注意的是，想法或兴趣一定是要与工程相关的，另外要将你的想法和康奈尔的研究相联系，这就要求申请者对康奈尔有关工程方面的研究有一定的了解。了解的方式很多，可以是通过网络获得资料，也可以是从朋友老师那里打听到，申请者在动笔写之前必须自己下些工夫。最后，解释康奈尔的工程教育对你有何意义，这就是一个普通的问题了，很多的学校都有这样的要求，让学生认识到他或她有可能就读的大学究竟对他们的人生有什么样的意义。
题目4
School of Hotel Administration: What work and non-work experiences, academic interests, and career goals influenced your decision to study hospitality management? How will these contribute to your success at the School of Hotel Administration?
酒店管理学院题目：是什么让你决定学习酒店管理？这些将在哪些方面有助于你今后的学习发展？
题目5
College of Human Ecology: What do you value about the College of Human Ecology perspective and the majors that interest you, as you consider your academic goals and plans for the future?
人类生态学院题目：当你想到学术目标和未来发展时，你怎么看待人类生态学院和你感兴趣的专业？
题目6
School of Industrial and Labor Relations: Describe your intellectual interests, their evolution, and what makes them exciting. In your essay please address how the ILR curriculum will help you fulfill these interests and your long-term goals.
工业和劳动关系学院题目：请描述你的学术兴趣、原因以及发展。请说明工业和劳动关系学院的课程将如何帮助你实现目标。
分析
以上三个题目都是类似的问题，我就把它们归到一起进行说明。对于以上的这些问题，千万要避免说大而空的话，比如说“英语是我最喜欢的科目”这一类的句子。相反的，我们需要做的是聚焦于细节、聚焦于英语的某个吸引你的特性。学院有没有某一个项目让你狂热？你喜不喜欢某个特定的写作风格？你的职业规划是什么？而你就读的专业跟这个相符吗？康奈尔大学的申请者在学术上都有很高的成绩，在学校表现优秀。而每个学院对于申请者又有一些细微的差异（成绩、高中课程），写作前在网上查查看你是否满足这一学院的要求。
