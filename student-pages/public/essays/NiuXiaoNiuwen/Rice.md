十五　Rice University 莱斯大学
学生背景
小张同学的确是一名特别的学生，说其特别是因为他由对音乐的喜爱转到了对理工科的兴趣。从小时候起他都不像别的孩子那样去背乐谱，一首曲子只要听到别人弹过一次，他就能在脑海中将不同音调转换成不同颜色的方块记下来，即使是完全陌生的曲调。这种将形象思维迅速转化为抽象思维的能力使他对音乐、对理工课程都有自己独到的理解方式。而莱斯大学不仅是一个以理工科为基础的大学，它的音乐学院也是美国大学的佼佼者，所以莱斯简直是为小张同学的量身定做的理想大学。
案例
题目1
Short Answer Questions
With the understanding that the choice of academic school you indicated is not binding, explain why you are applying to that particular school of study.
虽然我们理解你现在对学院的选择并无法律约束力，但我们想知道你为什么会申请到这个学院学习。
If you have applied or expect to apply to other colleges or universities this year, please name them.
如果你今年已经申请或希望申请其他学院或大学，请列出它们的名称。
What motivated you to apply to Rice University? Please be specific and limit your response to 200 words.
是什么促使你申请莱斯大学？请谈得具体点（不超过200字）。
分析
莱斯大学位于美国得克萨斯州的休斯敦市，其规模虽小却有南方哈佛之称，其工程系尤为出名。另外，相对于美国其他私立高等学府，莱斯的学费绝对具有号召力，被称为是性价比极高大学。
莱斯大学的申请题目其实和其他很多大学差别不大。几个问题归结到一起，无非是问申请者，你为什么想来莱斯大学？你为什么选择学习这个专业？回答这样的问题切忌说些大而空的套话，而是要讲到具体的点上。题目中也明确提出要求，要谈得具体点。莱斯大学录取时最看重的就是“fit”，要大学与人相匹配。申请者的表述要让校方认为你就是他们要的人，莱斯大学就是为你量身定制的，具体内容要从你自己的人生经历以及对未来的规划中获得。
最后，注意莱斯大学的题目中有一个特殊的要求，就是要求申请者列出当年申请的其他学校的名称。其实也是希望从中看到你真正的志向和想法。所以申请应尽量列出与莱斯大学在各个方面都比较相似的学校。
范文
谈到自己对物理的兴趣逐渐发展成对工程的热爱，工程是将理论应用于实践的学科，自己想成为一名工程师。因为我们的生活离不开工程师的工作：设计桥梁、隧道、堤坝和各种基础设施以及交通运输、商业、能源和工业工程领域中的问题。
I always have a dream of becoming an engineer in the future. My passion for engineering actually grows from my love of physics. Every twist and turn, every puzzle and solution in physics seem like profound magic to me. The core value of physics is to study and research ideas, while the core value of engineering is to utilize and realize them. The idea of "make true" has been fascinating me for a long time, and engineering always enchants me. Nothing around our life can do without the work of engineers: the designing of bridges, tunnels, dams and all kinds of infrastructure for transportation, commerce, energy and industry are matters in the engineering field. It seems that engineering can create anything and solve any problem. In this sense, engineers are in no difference with magicians, and I am willing to make my own magic in the future.
为了提高我们的生活质量，我们需要开发新技术和改善目前的状况。我们生存的世界存在很多问题，如，气候变化、能源枯竭等。我很关心这些问题，希望可以找到解决方案，确保可持续发展。
The world is facing an increased demand from a growing population. The sustainability of urban systems and infrastructure are challenged by rapid changes both from human activities and the nature. To improve the quality of our life, we need to develop new technologies and improve the current situation. The problems of energy shortage, climate change and environment pollution are closely tied to every one of us. I am concerned about these problems, for we have to find solutions and creations for the current and future generations to sustain and develop. For example, it's said that coal, the main source of burning fuel we depend heavily on today, is to run out in several decades. So it's the engineers' duty to figure out a way to keep our lamps burning. Hard as it seems, engineers will find solutions.
表达自己献身工程事业的决心，莱斯大学和自己是绝对匹配的。
I am determined to devote myself into engineering, and I am ready to take all the challenges. Great engineers, I believe, will need not only skills and knowledge, but also visions and perspectives to the world. In Rice, I am confident that I will finally have the eyes, hands and mind for a great engineer.
分析
文章的结构大致可分三个部分。第一部分，谈到自己对工程的兴趣，将来想成为一名工程师；第二部分，讲到工程可以改变世界，工程师对于我们生存的世界和未来至关重要；第三部分，表达自己献身工程事业的决心，莱斯大学是自己实现梦想的首选。总体上讲，完全符合莱斯大学的录取要求：“fit”。没有惊天动地的经历，也没有华丽夸张的表述，同样能赢得青睐。
题目2
Essay
The Committee on Admission is interested in getting to know each candidate as well as possible through the application process. The following essay question is designed to demonstrate your writing skills and facilitate our full appreciation of who you are.
Architecture applicants should skip (A) and respond to (B).
录取委员会的人希望尽可能多地了解每一位申请者。下边的问答意在展示你的写作技能，同时帮助我们更好地了解你的其他情况。
建筑学专业应跳过（A）直接回答（B）。
A. The quality of Rice's academic life and the Residential College System are heavily influenced by the unique life experiences and cultural traditions each student brings. What perspective do you feel that you will contribute to life at Rice?(Most applicants are able to respond successfully in two to three double-spaced pages.)
每一个学生独特的个人经历与文化传统都会对莱斯大学的学术生活质量和大学寄宿体制产生巨大的影响。你觉得你对莱斯大学的生活将会有什么贡献？（双倍行距，2～3页）
分析
大学之所以为大学，不仅因为它传授给学生专业的技能和知识，更重要的是它在每个学生的心里播下了人文情怀的种子，这份对人类社会的情愫和关怀是我们每个人都应当具备并为之自豪的。莱斯大学的这个题目就是想从另一个侧面了解申请者除了智商和知识以外的个人素质，比如爱好、特长等，这些都能反映到自己生活的社区，自己的行为对生活在你周围的人产生什么样的影响，这是很重要的。自己人格的完整性不仅会对自己的人生产生重大影响，对自己周围的人和事亦如此。
范文
开篇即点出自己想去Shepherd，继续自己的音乐家之路。
It was like a delicate dance to maintain my piano playing in a high level while at the same time keep a perfect school record. Now as I walk close to the door of Shepherd, I want to continue on my path to become a great musician.
自己认为的音乐家应该具有的特质是完整的人格、丰富的想象力、渊博的知识和创造力。此外，语言学习和旅游同样能激发音乐家的灵感。而这恰恰是Shepherd能给予我的。
A complete musician with complete personality and all the imagination, knowledge and creativity, is what I want to become. A diverse intellectual and aesthetic education gives me wings to fly higher and see further in my music performance. Language learning and travels are also springs of inspirations for musician. I love Shepherd because it can provide me the optimal environment for such a life fully absorbed in the creation and performance of music.
浅谈自己对音乐的感悟，并指出Shepherd将会以其独有的特质把自己塑造成真正的音乐家。
It is a highly educated mind with the intensive knowledge and the extensive perspectives of this world that helps create great music and true musician. Without such abundant performances, exposure to difference, interactions with masterminds and constant involvement in the community as a musician, our music will disappear gradually. Here in Shepherd, I believe my curiosity to the new and courage to brave through the bad can be encouraged and cultivated, so as to impart in me not just agility of fingers or sharpness of ears, but a full-blown musician in hearts and minds.
分析
本文作者热爱音乐，特别擅长钢琴。在文中作者谈了自己对音乐的理解，对一名音乐家的理解。在作者心目中，一个完美的、大气的音乐家，不仅仅是耳聪目明，眼疾手快，还必须拥有完整的人格，丰富的知识和想象力以及不竭的创造力。好的音乐与周围的世界密不可分，丰富的音乐人生也离不开身边的人和事。作者用自己对音乐的感悟，娓娓道出了作者的人格魅力，给大家留下了很大的想象空间。身边有着一个对音乐和人生有如此感悟的人，你的生活必定更加精彩。本文无形中回答了题目涉及的问题。
题目3
Essays for Architecture Applicants Only
建筑专业学生必写
B. Please respond to each of the following: 1) What aspirations, experiences, or relationships have motivated you to pursue the study of architecture? 2) Outside of academics, what do you enjoy most or find most challenging? (Responses to each section should be approximately one page.)
请对如下问题作答：1）是什么愿望、经历或关系促使你想学习建筑学？2）除了学习之外你最喜欢什么或者什么对你最有挑战性？
分析
这个题目跟其他专业的题目并没有本质上的区别，只是作答的范围仅限于建筑学领域而已，申请者应当好好把握。
