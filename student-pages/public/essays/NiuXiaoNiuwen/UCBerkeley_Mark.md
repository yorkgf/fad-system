学生背景
Mark一直在美国读书，父母均是企业高管，后来自己经商。Mark虽然没有太多特别的经历，但是一次网球失败的经历对他的成长影响很大。那次网球比赛因受到不公正待遇，Mark输掉了比赛。但是他并没有怨天尤人，而是深刻反省自我。他意识到自己在比赛中过于循规蹈矩，于是不断调整自己，让自己去适应不同的对手和不同的环境。结果，在另一场比赛中，面对不同的环境和不同的对手，Mark如愿赢得了比赛。还有一次让他印象深刻的经历是自己转到国际高中后，在学习上由于过于自满而沦为全班垫底。意识到自己的错误后，Mark及时调整状态，严格要求自己，坚持不懈地努力学习，终于成为班里名列前茅的学生。他很珍惜这次教训，并将其用于以后的学习、工作、生活中。这样一个不服输、敢于拼搏、能够适应任何环境变化、面对挑战的学生当然会赢得伯克利的青睐。
案例
题目1
Describe the world you come from—for example, your family, community or school—and tell us how your world has shaped your dreams and aspirations. 500 words
描述你生长的地方，如你的家庭、社区或学校。你成长的世界如何塑造了你的梦想和理想？（不超过500字）
分析
要写好一篇大学入学申请的文章，首先，不要直接写你经历了什么悲惨的事或你曾经战胜了什么困难。原因很简单，所有的申请者都在写同一个题目，每个人都有自己的故事。如何才能在众多的文章中脱颖而出呢？要选取一些貌似很普通的事件来写，正是在这些普通的事件中让人认识到你是什么样的人，这样留给对方的印象才会深刻。
其次，就是关于写故事手法的问题。要让故事呈现在对方面前，你的描述要让读到故事的人似乎亲自见证了故事的发生，这需要有很好的功底。每所大学都非常期望有这样的学生。
最后，具体到某一个时刻，而不是泛泛地描述你的人生。用短短的500字不可能完整描述你的人生或你生活的世界，但总有那么一个时刻，你感动了，你成长了，这样的时刻定义了你是谁。选取这样的时刻，简单而准确描述它，让对方能感受到你的品质和修养，这比直接告诉对方要好很多。要是能体现一些个性和创新性，那就更完美了。
范文
我出生于经商世家，从小就受到父母的悉心教导，耳濡目染，使自己对商业产生了极大兴趣。这种好奇心也时时驱使我去进一步了解这个世界和商业知识。
Growing up in a Chinese "business family" has made me the determined young man I am today. My parents, both successful commercial leaders, have facilitated my study of management and economics since early childhood. Our dinner conversations have always comprised vivid accounts of complex transactions or global financial trends, and my mom and dad always met my naïve questions with patient explanations of the principles involved. Curiosity has long driven me to learn more about this world, and I have been relentless in my pursuit of business knowledge.
爸爸对我影响很大，他不吝啬将他对人、对市场以及对物品的想法与我分享，教给我如何将经济学的理论与实际结合。此段作者详细讲述了自己从十几岁时就开始跟爸爸学习经商的经历。
My dad has been especially influential in my amateur apprenticeship. As a regional manager of a multinational company and a veteran of numerous state-owned and international enterprises, he has generously shared his insights into people, markets and materials, teaching me how economic and financial theory intersect with actual practice. At age twelve, I read Alfred Marshall's Principles of Economics with his urging, and I frequently accompanied him throughout junior high school to events with clients and colleagues. I sat in on my first board meeting as a note-taker when I was fifteen, wide-eyed and keenly observant. From these valuable opportunities, I learned countless details about business cultures, industrial operations, and the nuances of contract negotiations. I relished these early glimpses into the role of global merchant that I hope to occupy one day.
进入高中后，我将自己对商业的热情更多地转换为实践。本段讲述了我的初步商业实践活动，并指出自己想通过学习以更多地掌握商业知识和方法。
During high school, I have translated my enthusiasm for business into more engaged, hands-on modes of learning. I completed two Advanced Placement courses in Economics and Business in order to firm up my theoretical foundations. I began actively researching companies that interested me, such that scanning financial charts and management reports now ranks among my favorite hobbies. With my mom's guidance, I also opened my own stock trading account and gradually ventured to pick my own trades rather than following advice from talking heads on television. Internships with a Swiss metal merchant and a high-end Chinese book retailer have allowed me to navigate communications, statistical analysis, and leadership as applied in an office environment. As my contact with business broadens, I am more anxious than ever to pursue higher education in this area and to learn about new industries and approaches.
阐述了爸爸取得成功的原因。为磨炼自己，我去欧洲和亚洲旅行使自己接触不同的文化，还上国际高中以更好地学习英语。这一次次的实践经历都使作者更加自信。
Exposure to business also ignited my passion for international exchange and global awareness. My father's success depends largely on his ability to befriend people from all walks of life, whether customers or suppliers, and that process requires a genuine interest in what other people believe and value. To hone this interest, I have traveled extensively throughout Europe and Asia, exposing myself to the cultural lessons available in each location. In addition, I chose to attend an international high school with instruction in English to ensure bilingual fluency. I know I have far to go before I secure my true niche
 in the world of business. But my parents, teachers and mentors have helped me understand what tools I will need to earn my spot, and I am confident I will get there.
分析
作者娓娓叙述了自己从小学到现在的成长环境，每一个阶段都围绕同一个主题来写，那就是一个在商业家庭环境中成长的孩子的典型。从孩童时期的耳濡目染，到后来父母的循循善诱和悉心教导，再到自己的学徒生涯和商业实践，再到对商业世界的热爱和接受相关高等教育的渴望。作者描述了自己在商业环境中的成长，一次次认识的变化造就了今天的他，将自己的这一面很好地呈现在了大家面前。
作者的英语水平无疑是相当不错的，很多表达非常生动形象，给人留下深刻的印象。如hone this interest，secure my true niche in the world of business，作者对动词的使用非常传神。
题目2
Tell us about a personal quality, talent, accomplishment, contribution or experience that is important to you. What about this quality or accomplishment makes you proud, and how does it relate to the person you are? 500 words
告诉我们一项你认为重要的品质、才能、成就、贡献或一次经历。这些都如何让你感到自豪，如何能体现你是怎样的人？（500字）
分析
题目的范围包括写自己的品质、才能、成就、贡献或一次经历。这给了你很大的选择空间。但重要的是这些品质也好，才能也好，经历也好，必须是你认为对你很重要的，而且使你感到自豪，更重要的是能反映出你是一个什么样的人。
那究竟选什么写呢？问问自己，什么才能、成就、贡献或经历能够最好地表达出自己最重要的人格品质？试着能不能用一个词来描述它，为什么这样描述是得体的？可以写下几个自己问自己的小问题的答案，将其一一列出，然后思考，比较出哪一个能最好地满足这个要求。然后再动笔写初稿。
范文
从一次网球比赛的失败中，我意识到勇于直面失败远比成功更有益，这让我有能力去反思、分析进而去完善自我。这个特征决定了我的性格和我的世界——不管我们做什么，能够灵活应对挑战是成功的必备条件。
Losses that expose our flaws teach us much more than victories that mask them. Since I learned this lesson on a tennis court in 2010, I have cultivated a response to failure that is perhaps my greatest strength: the capacity to reflect, analyze and improve my performance, whatever unexpected obstacle I might discover. This trait has become a defining aspect of both my character and my worldview. No matter what we are doing, the ability to react nimbly to challenges is absolutely essential to success.
在经历了一次失败后，我静心苦练三年，终于又迎来了一次比赛，与对手短兵相接使我的心怦怦直跳，但是，当球碰触球网的那一刻，我很快进入了比赛状态。
It began for me in a lonely moment during the quarterfinals of the Kappa-CRT China Open Tennis Tournament. After three years of hard training, I faced a match point for my opponent and the threat of elimination. My heart pounded as I bounced from foot to foot, anticipating the serve. With the thwack of ball against racquet, I sprang into action.
裁判宣布：王6∶2领先，王赢了比赛。
"The set goes to Wang, 6-2. Wang wins the match.”
当裁判宣布对方胜利的时候，我的心情低落到了极点。我一度将这次失败归结为自己太不走运，但是随着时间的推移，我认识到，是自己训练准备中的错误方法导致了失败。因为一直以来我只和教练对抗练习，熟知教练的打球套路，从未学习变换不同策略。所以，当面对陌生对手的时候，自己就不知道如何调整策略。由此，我总结出，适应性不仅对生活很重要，对网球比赛亦如此。
When I heard the referee declare my opponent's victory, I sank to my knees in despair. My confidence shattered, I tried to convince myself it was just bad luck. Over time, however, I realized that the fault lay in my preparation. Because I always practiced against my coach, I knew just what to expect each time he struck the ball. His style persisted from match to match, so while I gained fitness and technique, I never learned to think strategically. When faced with unfamiliar opponents at the tournament, I did not know how to adjust my tactics. The moral of the story was a simple one: the world rarely serves the ball exactly where you want it. Unexpected events are the norm rather than the exception, so adaptability is essential as part of preparation for life as well as tennis matches.
当我有了正确的认识后，经过不断改进，在以后的比赛中，我屡次取得胜利。我不仅组建了自己的乐队，还在一个正在调整运营模式的公司实习，以此见证商业中如何运用调整性。
Since coming to that realization, I have sought to apply this wisdom to all my everyday activities. For example, I returned to the tennis court determined to study the game with new savvy（n.悟性、理解能力）, and in 2010, I won doubles in my division at the Kappa-CRT tournament. That same year, careful training earned me the Most Improved Player honors on our championship basketball team and numerous prizes at Beijing's prestigious music festival. I also recruited several friends to form a band named The Donkey Riders, and though our playing styles clashed initially, we managed to become skilled and popular through rigorous rehearsal. I sought an internship last summer with a company I knew was overhauling its business model so I could witness how adaptation works at a commercial level.
一次次的经历证明，比赛的残酷性就是要提高参赛者的不同特质，满足现状必然导致失败。回顾往事，我希望自己能够继续那场十分之一决赛，但是我也很高兴自己输掉了那场比赛，因为那次失败让自己学到了受益终身的东西。
Each of these experiences has only confirmed for me that the relentless search for opportunities to improve is what distinguishes us within a crowd of competitors. Being comfortable with the status quo is a guarantee of failure. In retrospect, part of me wishes I had rallied to take that quarterfinal match. Another part of me, however, is glad it went my opponent's way and helped me learn a lesson that will follow me forever.
分析
作者写了自己一次比赛失败的经历，从中悟出的一个其受用终身的道理。那就是在面对新的困难和挑战时，要学会调整策略，随机应变，事情不会总是按事先的规划来发展的。
作者的英文水平非常不错，对事件的描述非常形象生动，用词也很准确。其中不少表达令人称道，如：With the thwack（n./vi. 啪的一声、强打）of ball against racquet, I sprang into action（指突然活跃起来），又如I sank to my knees in despair. My confidence shattered（adj.破碎的）……这些描述都给人很强的画面感，令人有身临其境、感同身受的感觉。
题目3
Additional Comments (optional)附加题目（选做）
If you wish, you may use this space to tell us anything else you want us to know about you that you have not had the opportunity to describe elsewhere in the application. 500 words
如果愿意，你可以在这里告诉我们在其他地方没有表达的内容。（限500字）
分析
这类选做题之前也多次提到。记住，这样的题目仍然是校方设计来了解申请者的，其目的在于给申请者一个机会表达在其他地方没有表达的内容，从而从另一个侧面了解申请者。写这类题目，注意内容不要重复。
范文
讲述了去年夏天自己取得了进步最快奖的荣誉，一年前自己还是班里垫底的学生，但是现在已经名列前茅了。
This past summer, four students stood before their peers to receive the "High Honors" designation. The principal's voice rang through the hall: "This student has made the fastest progress of anyone in the school's history. Comparing his results from last year and this year is like contrasting night and day." Just a year before, my poor performance had ranked me near the bottom of my class. Now, I was at the top.
高二的时候，我从当地的中文学校转到了一所国际高中。以前的学校只注重期末考试而不是评估，而我总是能轻易地脱颖而出，因而来这里之后我自信满满地认为我能适应任何学校的学习环境。
In my sophomore year of high school, I transferred from a local Chinese school to an international one. I had always excelled easily in the Chinese system, which relied largely on end-of-semester exams rather than frequent evaluations. I was confident that I could succeed in any school environment.
我的自信逐渐转为自满，这种心态甚至付诸了行动。我没有花经历去调整自己，而是利用早退的时间使自己沉浸在课外活动中。很快，我就成为篮球队、网球队、音乐话剧俱乐部的领导人员。我甚至还养成了拖延交作业的坏习惯。当第一次评估报告出来的时候，我认识到自己为这些坏习惯付出了沉重的代价。
My attitude translated into complacency, which quickly compromised my performance. I did not pay much attention to how I needed to adapt but rather took advantage of the early dismissal time to immerse myself in extracurricular activities. It did not take me long to become a leader on the basketball and tennis teams and in music and drama clubs. I developed a bad habit of procrastinating on projects. When my first report card arrived, I paid for my mistakes.
我不敢相信自己居然在家庭作业中失败了，这篇报告不能带自己去理想的大学。我意识到自己必须做出改变。
I could not believe I had failed to succeed in my schoolwork. That report card would not take me anywhere I wanted to go, much less to my dream university. I had to change.
面对现实，我给自己制订了一个严格的学习计划，每天都要求自己按时完成计划中的学习任务。到升高三审查考试的时候，我的物理、数学都取得了满分，而且把自己高二时落下的课程全都补上来了。
With a set of realistic but ambitious goals, I set myself a harsh study schedule. Throughout the summer, I conformed to it, no matter how many distractions threatened my focus. I often stayed up until two or three o'clock in the morning to finish my work, drinking tea or coffee whenever I felt tired to guide me through my daily "to-do" list. My diligence paid off, and when I took the review exams at the beginning of my junior year, I scored full marks in physics and math, showing I had caught up on all of the work I had shirked as a sophomore.
我很珍惜自己学到的这一点，并将其运用于以后的学习、工作和家庭中。我不再自满和自负，而是学会了随时准备调整自己，为不断追求卓越而做出牺牲，为实现目标不断奋斗。只有这样，才能成为真正的领导，成为别人引以为傲的典范。
The culmination of my hard work came at that ceremony in June, when the principal drew everyone's attention to my uncanny trajectory. It is a day I will always cherish. More importantly, the recognition I earned represents a deeper set of values that will translate from high school to college to career, family and community, informing every action and every decision of my life going forward. Never again will I take the path of complacency and overconfidence. In every instance, I will prepare myself optimally, sacrifice for excellence, and push myself to achieve all that I am able to. In this way, I know I will establish myself as a leader and an exemplar others will be proud to follow.
分析
作者写了自己获得最佳进步奖的经历，从一个班级垫底到成绩优异的蜕变过程。通过对选做题目的写作，展现在校方面前的是另一个自己，是一个不服输、敢于拼搏、能够适应任何环境变化、面对挑战的人。
