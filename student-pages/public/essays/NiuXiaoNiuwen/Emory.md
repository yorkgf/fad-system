十六　Emory University 埃默里大学
学生背景
小岳同学来自北京，有着广泛的兴趣爱好。他在网球方面有很高的造诣，曾拿过数次冠军。书法也是小岳的强项，小时候家里的墙壁都成了他的画纸。小岳还是一名很好的男低音，在各种歌唱比赛中屡次获奖。大学里的小岳不仅继续着自己个人爱好，作为学校学生会外联部主席的他还带领整个外联部为学校和同学们做出了很多贡献。为此，校方还给他颁发了奖牌。但是，当他得知这个奖只是为他一人而发，没有部里其他成员的时候，他毅然拒绝上台领奖。小岳认为这个奖是全体外联部成员努力的结果，自己虽然身为主席更不能独领此奖，这项荣誉是属于大家的。这件事不仅显示出小岳卓越的领导能力，更深刻体现了他的团队精神和公共服务意识。埃默里大学是一个注重社区回报和公共服务意识的学校，小岳同学的这种精神正是学校孜孜不倦去追求的。所以，小岳同学被录取也是情理之中。
案例
题目1
Short Essay Questions
Beyond your academic and extracurricular accomplishments, the essay section allows you to communicate who you really are and share any information you would like the admission committee to consider. If you are applying to Emory College, Oxford College, or both, keep this in mind as you craft your answers to the essay questions.
除你的学术和课业成就之外，小文章部分希望你能提供任何你认为录取委员会的人可能考虑的信息，让大家认识真实的你。如果你是申请埃默里学院、牛津学院或两个都申请，在写文章时千万别忘了两点：交流和真实的你！
Required Essay:
What are the unique qualities of Emory University, and the specific school(s) to which you are applying (Emory College of Arts and Sciences, Oxford College, or both), that make you want to become part of Emory University? In what ways do you hope to take advantage of the qualities you have identified? Please limit your response to 250 words.
埃默里大学和你申请的学院的什么特质吸引了你，想成为他们的一部分？（埃默里文理学院、牛津学院或两者兼有）你打算如何利用你所提到的特质？（不超过250字）
分析
同样的问题，Why Emory? Why you? 这里不仅要找出埃默里大学的特质、优点，更重要的是要让他们了解你的特质和优点，同时说服他们，埃默里大学最适合你，你也适合埃默里大学。
写埃默里大学的特质和优点，首先就要了解埃默里大学。实地考察对很多人来说可能不太现实，但问问周围的朋友老师，上互联网搜索查找些相关资料，这应该没有问题。另外，非常重要的一点是，挖掘你自己的特质和优点，并与埃默里大学匹配。当然，千万注意别和之前自己提到的东西重复了，别忘了，这是补充性essay。最后，特别再说一点，写文章的时候注意一定要简洁，不能长篇大论。
范文
开篇点出埃默里大学的暑期项目和丰富多彩的校园生活深深地吸引着我，并促使我竭力去申请该校。
When considering my future development, I choose to pursue academic fulfillment, which make Emory University suit me most. Undoubtedly that the remarkable educational quality in EU fixes my attention, but for me, the advanced summer programs and colorful life form significant factors in pushing me to work hard in applying for EU.
埃默里大学的课外学习项目不仅教给了学生知识，提高了学生处理复杂问题的能力，更重要的是给学生的人生留下了难忘的一笔。
In EU, we have the honor to take various curricula taught by faculties with global perspectives, and we will be more comprehensively improved in choosing and attending its Domestic Transient Study at Puerto Rico or U.S Virgin Islands. With such unforgettable experiences, we can have a deeper understanding of different cultures and international affairs, which will sharp our mind and cultivate our capacity of dealing with tough issues, meaning more than the knowledge in books.
从埃默里大学学生的微笑中，我看到了他们良好的精神面貌和对生活的热爱，而这恰恰就是自己想要的。我对生活的热情和乐观，使我相信自己不仅能在埃默里大学提升自己，还能为学校社区的建设做出贡献。
Deeply lighted by the smiles of the EU students, I find that life in EU is full of fresh, joy and challenges. It fascinates me a lot in talking with friends from different races on the grass; it also gives me enjoyment in taking in some students' art centers or sports teams. With a broad range of hobbies, I possess great enthusiasm toward life, which gives me the eye to find even small beauty in some ordinary corners. That enthusiasm and optimism make me suitable to the wonderful life in Emory. As a team person who is looking forward to embracing all the challenges, I am confident that I can flourish in EU and make contributions to its community.
分析
文章开篇提到了埃默里大学有非常高的教学质量，但最吸引作者的是他们的夏季项目和丰富多彩的校园生活。第二段接第一段继续讲到埃默里大学的课外学习项目，这样的学习项目不仅教给了学生知识，更重要的是给经历者的人生留下了难忘的一笔。这些都很具体地提到了埃默里大学的优势和特点。看得出来，作者在申请前是花了工夫去了解埃默里大学的。最后一段提到了埃默里大学学生的笑脸，从他们的笑脸看到了他们良好的精神面貌和对生活的热爱，这正是作者自己想要的，学校有的和自己要的天然地配在了一起。
题目2
Optional Essay (if you choose not to submit this essay, it will not affect your admission decision)
选择性题目（如果你不做本题目将不会影响对你的录取决定）
分析
这是一则选择性的题目，明确说明了如不做该题目将不会影响录取的结果。这是不是就说明我们可以不用管他了呢？当然不是。实际上，最后的录取结果受到了什么因素的影响谁也说不好。如果完全没有用，又何必给你这样一个选做题呢？所以，申请者应当抓住每一个可以展现自己的机会，尽量多把自己优秀的东西呈现在录取委员会的面前。
所以，一定要选取典型的题材，能体现自己某方面优秀品质和才能的东西来写，这样才能出彩，赢得大家的青睐。
范文
在被宣布赢得最佳创意奖和最佳服务奖之后，我转过身默默地离开了领奖大厅。
"Wow, the next department is a big winner with two awards! The awards of best originality and best service go to—the Publicity Department of Student Union!" Amid enthusiastic applause, I turned away quietly and left the hall without looking back. "Chief of the department, Chen Yangyang, please come up to the stage to..." Those words disappeared behind me with the lightest trace ever.
解释了自己没有上台领奖的原因：我认为这些奖是我们团队合作的成果，虽然自己是团队的领导，但是自己不应该独自获得此奖项。因为对这个颁奖感到失望，我拒绝接受这个奖项。
This is said to be the most unforgettable moment at the Commendation Ceremony of Student Union this year. I am no diva, nor trying to be famous by making a scene. This was a silent protest against the union's decision of giving awards to me, only to me, the leader of the whole department. What I had in mind was for all my team members to go on stage and receive the awards, the acknowledgment and encouragement together as a team. The school nicely rejected my request, nor did it allow me to read the names of my team members on stage. Ironically, it was their painstaking efforts in every project that won these awards for our department, yet they were not allowed to share the glory? The student union was determined to give awards to department leaders, even if some of them didn't actually shoulder their due responsibilities. Disappointed at the decision, I decided to decline receiving the awards.
团队合作是我最为重视的。作为领导，一个最易忽视的责任就是确保所有的成员得到相应的奖励。我曾遇到一个极大的挑战：帮助学生会准备罗马尼亚文化节。所有团队成员被分配一个罗马尼亚城市，他们各自设计一本小册子，并绘制一张海报。最终我们的工作赢得了一致的好评，这个文化节被形容为高娱乐性和高信息量。
As a leader, I believe in unusual vision, resolute action, and most importantly, great teamwork. An often overlooked responsibility of the leader is to ensure that all members get their due rewards. But seeing the unreserved devotion of members of our department, anyone will agree it was most unfair not to give them some credits. Sacrificing their leisure time for tasks of the department has become familiar to every member in the Publicity Department. More often than not, the project is so urgent with many time-consuming tasks that they have to stay up late to meet the deadline. As the chief director, I feel guilty that I know so little about painting. Fortunately, my team members take great initiative to take on demanding tasks for me, and for other departments that need our aid. Last year, our department met one of the biggest challenges when the student union asked us to take charge of the preparation for the coming Romanian Culture Festival in our school. The workload was huge. All the members on team were assigned each a Romanian city, about whom they should design a brochure and draw a poster. With earnest efforts and late hours into nights, we managed to finish the work ahead of schedule. Finally, our work won unanimous acclaim and the festival was described as entertaining and highly informative.
学生会在自己的领导下不断发展壮大，并成功举办20多次校园活动，每个会员在活动中都很认真负责。
The Publicity Department of the Student Union had only nine members when I first joined it. Through these years, we have absorbed twenty one more members with my leadership, our vitality and cohesion. We have held over twenty major campaigns on campus, all of which turned out to be very successful. From conceiving to determining the themes, from publicizing to the final implementation, we were responsible for every key process.
简单总结，我坚信自己不上台领奖的行为是正确的。
Looking back on those days when all team members worked arduously day and night, I believe I did the right thing turning down the awards that were only given to me personally. As rebellious as it may seem, this is what I believe a good leader should do.
分析
作者整篇文章写了自己的一次拒绝上台领奖的经历。原因是作者认为获此殊荣是他们整个团队精诚合作、努力拼搏的结果，而组织者却把奖颁给了他个人，所以他不能接受。
通过对这样一件事情的叙述，作者很好地体现了自己的一些品质，如领导能力和团队精神。因颁奖给个人而拒绝接受自然体现了作者的团队精神，而他们团队的成绩正好反映了作者的领导能力。这是非常重要的品质，通过这样的方式传递到录取委员会面前，这无疑会给自己的申请加分，谁又能说选做题不会影响最终的结果呢？
题目3
Please write exactly five sentences that best describe you.
请用五句话描述你自己。
分析
这也是选做题目之一，道理和上边一样，虽是选做，但要尽量选，尽量做好。这个题目其实挺简单，但是要写出新意来就不那么容易了。有人建议多用长句，每句都写成几行，交上去也差不多一页多了，其实大可不必，这样反而容易引起对方的反感。写这样的题目一定要简洁，五句话可写自己五个不同的特点。校方出任何题目都是为了多了解申请者。所以，你要问问自己，还有什么是你想告诉对方的，想好了告诉对方就可以了。
那么，何来新意呢？你不妨在句式上下点工夫，比如五个排比句，五个相互连贯的句子等。
范文
Being observant, I always want to learn more with curiosity.
With logic thinking, I can find the correct direction in the ocean of researches.
Known for diligence, I embrace academic challenges and intend to work them out.
Caring about others, I am a perfect team person and will bring joy wherever I go.
Born aspiring, I have the confidence to pursue excellence.
分析
作者用极其简短的五句话描述了自己五个方面的特质，善于观察，有较强的逻辑思维能力，勤奋努力，关心身边的人以及志向远大。五句话句式极为相似，整齐而富有美感。
