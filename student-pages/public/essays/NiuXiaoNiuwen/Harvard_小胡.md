一　Harvard University 哈佛大学
学生背景
小胡同学一直很受老师喜欢，因为这位同学各方面都很优秀。爸爸经营公司，妈妈是科学家，家教一直都非常好。她的经历中，有几点比较显著。第一，她会六门外语：英语、法语、德语、西班牙语、拉丁语、韩语。第二，她去过很多国家。小学有一年在澳大利亚，中学有一年在英国，高中有一年在美国做交换生。第三，她爱好广泛，如篮球、小风琴。哈佛这个学校特点就是不太看重诸如你是否在学生会担任过什么职务之类的事情，它看重的是你个人如何卓尔不群。所以在写的时候关键要突出自己的想法，写出自己特立独行的特点。小胡从来都不要别人告诉她怎么做，她好多事情都是自己做。所以，文书就要突出她的与众不同，着笔可以集中在小事上。她以2100多分申请到哈佛，主要是由于她自身很符合这个学校的气质。
案例
题目
Additional Essays
Occasionally, students feel that college application forms do not provide sufficient opportunity to convey important information about themselves or their accomplishments. If there is something you would like us to know, please inform us below. If you wish to include an additional essay, you may do so.
有些学生认为大学申请表格没有提供足够的空间来让他们描述个人情况或既往成就。如果你有什么想让我们知道的，请将其填入下面的空格。如果你想附加一篇额外的文章，下面有相应的题目可供选择。
Possible Topics:
—Unusual circumstances in your life
—Travel or living experiences in other countries
—A letter to your future college roommate
—An intellectual experience (course, project, book, discussion, paper or research topic) that has meant the most to you
—How you hope to use your college education
—A list of the books you have read during the past twelve months
可供选择的主题：
——生活中不寻常的情况或事件
——在别国旅游或生活的经历
——写给未来大学室友的一封信
——对你影响最大的学术事件（课程、项目、图书、讨论、文章或研究课题）
——你希望如何利用你的大学教育
——在过去一年中你的读书清单
分析
哈佛大学一直是优秀学生的必争之地，每个人都以能进入哈佛为豪。近几年来哈佛大学的综合录取率一直在5%左右，而对于中国学生而言，每年40名本科生录取的数字就更加令人望而生畏了。每个学生都是极其优秀的，这个时候你面前势必有个老大难的问题：如何脱颖而出？！
在所有的学校申请过程中，一个基本原则就是做真我，哈佛申请也不例外。在这个额外文书面前，首先要确定这个文书是能给你加分的。动手之前，想想你在通用文书里面已经提到过的信息，然后在脑子里面想想还有什么其他信息可以用来告诉录取委员会的人，而这经历又是别人没有的。
特殊强调一下，在哈佛给的题目里面有一项是“请列出在过去一年中你的读书清单”。如果你之前用了大量时间来阅读学术方面的或让你深入思考的图书，这个清单势必会给你加分不少。但如果你的列表仅仅包括一些畅销书或心灵鸡汤一类的，那你最好换一个话题。
最后说一句，花时间把你最好的作品呈现在录取委员会面前。当你完成了第一稿后，想想阅读你这篇文章的人。一篇文章，四万左右申请者，这就意味着你必须写些有意义的东西才行！
范文
开篇引出自己的不同，想法跟别人不同，行为也跟别人不同。
I was in eyes of many a heretic, never ceasing to think differently and act differently. From very early on, I have found my perspectives toward the world we live in or the various concerns we have in life are somehow apart from that of others. Why is it the way things are? Could I ever do it in a different fashion? The world, with its immense grandeur and infinite boundary, seems to me a place alive with opportunities and wonders to be explored.
中国的教育，毫无例外，是一个古老和僵化的考试模式和书本记忆。尽管我不是很支持这种体制，但是初中和高中成绩相当好。我认为，我们的观点和看法要随着世界的变化而改变。
Schooling in China follows, with almost no exception, an ancient and rigid pattern of exam performance and memorization of texts. Even though I did not approve the system totally, I did quite well in normal junior high and senior high school classes, in almost every aspect that I delve into. In my mind, our views and perspectives should change along with the world, which is itself accelerating in terms of its own basis in culture, knowledge, human experience and even our water, air and food. How can a staid or passive view correctly rationalize or even describe the world and ourselves? I can never satisfy myself with such an approach.
我思考过很多事情，也尝试过好多。和我的大多数朋友们不同，我用一种非常规的方法挑战自己。在高中第一年，我开始大量阅读心理学和心理分析的文章，并进行了一项科研，将1100位高中生作为研究对象。
I tried many things and thought about many as well. Unlike many of my friends, I dared myself on somehow unconventional paths. In my first year of high school, I started to read extensively of Psychology and Psychoanalysis. When other students were busy writing summaries of their moral text reading digests, I followed the models in modern psychological research to conduct a survey of 1,100 high school students in my city, profiling them in terms of indices of mental health, pressure, parental/childhood influence, college goals and relationships with peers. I also tried my talents in business operations, as my family was successful in retailing business and it does have some positive impacts on me.
作者提到自己常写商业方面的评论。在今年5月，作者趁周末还到北京去搜集汽车产业的第一手资料。作者认为思想需要得到训练，否则我们生活就没有了意义。
Every week, I keep myself busy writing all sorts of business review articles, with special interests in real estate, retailing and automobile. My high school classes hardly touch upon such issues, while I have braced up courage to write to, or even sometimes bold enough to call professors, businessmen or CEOs in companies for interview or consultations. I kept close monitoring of various economic development data, stock market changes, fairs, expositions and lectures. In May this year, I went on a weekend to Beijing to gather the most valuable first-hand information on AUTO industry, on the way back from which I have already finished writing my reviews. I like writing, and I like to write on and on, especially the truth. Thoughts flow like streams sometimes when I write, so intensely burning in mind that my typing just would stop for good. The little "composer," was a nickname that I was called in school. I believe a pen can be easily found, but a mind of thoughts needs to be trained hard with practice and exposures. Otherwise, what do we live for?
作者的第一篇发表的文章是一篇评论，评论一位速食面条制造商在技术方面所做的改革，即将塑料碗改成纸碗。作者做出评论并提出质疑：产品的生产过程是否对食物有害？
I recall my first published article by a business magazine was a commentary on a technological reform by an instant noodle producer changing plastic to paper based containers. I commented on whether such a change was environmentally sound as generating pulp for such purposes were time and energy consuming. I further questioned if the production process is healthy to food processing if the making of the pulp for paper containers was regenerating and recycling.
写作的一个好处是通过自学掌握心理学和人类行为科学方面的知识。作者最后一句作了升华：只有当一个人深入到自己所擅长的领域，他才能真正谈得上改变了世界。
My article was a minor success and I had made many reader friends through this article. One great advantage that I have in writing these articles was my self-learned psychology and behavioral science. Using these methods to assess and evaluate human behaviors and psychological pursuits in market environment helped my paper writing a lot. I believe only when one deepens and strengthens what he does best can he truly claim to change the world.
提出选择哈佛的理由：1925年提出的商业案例分析法以及能将学生培养成现实而有远见的人。
I choose Harvard not simply because of the fame and name attached to a Harvard student, but more to some concrete reasons: her unique business case study teaching method in 1925 and her strength in training business students to be realistic and visionary. I have formed in myself such a thinking framework to focus on each case or instant's own particularity before making analysis. What Harvard does in class was what I have desired to have in mind for all these years. When wealth assembles or dissipates momentarily in our society, I want to know more the process and probe into the insides of the wealth generation process. I love the wealth and the creator of wealth, because wealth is powerful. But I do not adore wealth or its creator, because wealth can not purchase everything in this world. Business is a great and mesmerizing world for me and I love to explore it from Harvard.
再次提出对哈佛的喜爱之情。
Besides the great business tradition, I want to emphasize my affection to Harvard on her long and strong tie to China and generations of Chinese scientists, businessmen, entrepreneurs and scholars. The Harvard-Yenching Institute and various Sino-America student exchange and activities forums are amazing places for me to grow and work as a young cultural ambassador.
最后道出了自己的希望和真挚的热爱。
It is a stage of talents, a palace of knowledge and an altar of truth. I should confess I have the feeling of hugging the school, in a metaphorical way, at the moment I read of your school tenets enshrining truth and reason.
分析
作者没有使用哈佛提供的话题，而只是简单地谈论自己，但效果是明显的。作者的这篇自我简介把自己的方方面面都讲述得非常清楚，让评委会的人一眼就认定这位作者的创新和独立思考能力，而这点恰恰是哈佛所看重的。
作者开篇就说自己跟别人不同，从小就有去探索世界的欲望，表明了作者强烈的好奇心。第二段则说明在中国的教育体制下，她仍然学得很好。不是因为被逼无奈，而是因为对学习内容本身的兴趣。除了课堂学习，她课下还自学感兴趣的东西，写评论，做调研。在这其中，她不得不跟形形色色的人打交道，去练习写作。最后段落作者表明对哈佛的喜爱，在这里，她不是模模糊糊地说些冠冕堂皇的话，相反，她列出了学院“unique business case study teaching method in 1925”“various Sino-America student exchange and activities forums”这些实实在在的例子，让评委会的人看到她的诚意和信心。
