案例
Your answer to the following questions will be evaluated along with the essay you wrote as part of your application. We encourage you to take advantage of this additional opportunity to let us know about your intellectual and personal interests. Please limit each answer as indicated.
我们鼓励你利用这个撰写额外文书的机会让我们了解你的学术爱好和个人爱好。
The following questions are required for engineering and transfer
 applicants. Please limit your response to one to three paragraphs.
以下问题针对工程专业申请者和转专业学生。
题目1
(For Engineering Applicants Only) If you are applying to the Pratt School of Engineering, please discuss why you want to study engineering and why you would like to study at Duke.
（工程专业申请者）如果你想申请普拉特工程学院，请告诉我们你想攻读工程专业和选择杜克大学的原因。
分析
写作这样的文章，真实可信是首要原则。适当夸大是可以的，但一定要掌握好度，让录取委员会的人读来真实可信。你可以讲讲你深入阅读一本书的经历，也可以是受父亲的影响接触到了这个行业领域并以此有了深厚的兴趣等。挖掘属于自己的故事，才能做到与众不同。
题目2
(For Transfer Applicants Only) Consider the books, essays, poems, or journal articles you have read over the last year or two, either for school or leisure. Please discuss the way in which one of them has changed your understanding of the world, other people, or yourself.
（转专业学生）请回想在过去的一年或两年中你读过的图书、散文、诗歌或期刊文章，无论是学术的还是休闲的。有没有其中的一项改变了你对世界、他人或自己的看法，请具体说明。
分析
这个题目其实就是让申请者讲述一个故事。写作之前，想想之前看过的作品里面有没有对你产生极大影响的，不管是大的也好小的也罢，将它分享给录取委员会的人。一旦你开始讲述，请一定要强调它对你的影响。不同的事件不同的角度都可以被你拿来所用，比如我之前看的Walden给我的印象就挺深的。“报纸是什么？报纸就是无聊的人看的无聊的东西，一个人死了，两个人死了，死来死去的，说一次我就知道了，何必天天报道！铁轨是什么？铁轨是无数个枕木铺就而成，同时也是用工人的尸体铺就而成，为什么要修建它？因为人要旅游！为什么人要旅游，我们不需要旅游！”这就是梭罗的思想，看完之后你不会拍手称奇吗？难道不会引起你的思考吗？
范文
Waiting stone
The river has urged for ten thousand times,
But I walk backwards through the sand
Finally stand here waiting for you
I have been waiting for so long
Believing love not only means pursuit
Waiting more of the enjoyment of love
Even without mouth
Just simple and ugly shape
Stone made of flowing slurry
Being hot can scald hands
So does being cold
If only you could give me a hit
这是一首关于决心和爱情的诗歌。当河水卷走小石块和泥沙的时候，只有石头在那儿等候再次见面。石头永远在那儿，因为它相信爱不仅意味着追求，更在于等待，而等待就是一种享受。
This is my favorite poem. I have been seeking its profound connotation all along. After hard thinking, I gradually realized that it is a poem about determination and love. Through such a wonderful poem, a physical phenomenon was reflected. When river flows by washing small stones and sediment away, only the stone left there waiting for meeting again. The stone is always there and never change its original intention for it believes that love not only means pursuing but also waiting and that waiting is more of an enjoyment. A hit given by the water is a reward with which the stone would be more resolute.
现实生活中，当我们遭遇困难和沮丧的时候，这种精神同样有效。如果生活中有我们想要的，我们应该鼓起勇气和决心去为它而战或为它而等待。这首诗使我在自己感兴趣的领域更有耐心和决心。
Associate the poem with reality I think a lot about life and our attitudes towards it. It is perseverance that we see in the poem, which may work when we come across difficulty and frustrating. If we really love or feel passionate about something, it is worth the wait and difficulty. More often than not, life has more trouble to offer than thorough fair. But if we feel that something or someone is what we want for our lives, we will gather up enough courage and determination to fight or wait for it, even if it just gives us a hit in the end. This poem has made me a more patient and determined person in doing what I am interested in. It will continue to influence me in the future.
分析
想把一首诗歌说好不容易，作者在这里剑走偏锋、出其不意。文中的第一段描述了诗歌中的内在含义，而在第二段作者将其与生活联系在了起。说明这种不屈不挠的精神在现实生活中同样有效，如果生活中有我们想要追求的东西，我们应该鼓起勇气和决心去为它而战或是为它而等待。
题目3
The following questions are optional. If you choose to answer them, please do so on an additional page.
下面的问题是选答。
(For Arts and Sciences Applicants Only) If you are applying to Trinity College of Arts and Sciences, please discuss why you consider Duke a good match for you. Is there something in particular at Duke that attracts you? Please limit your response to one or two paragraphs.
（文理学院申请者）如果你申请三一学院（Trinity College）的文理学院，请说明为什么杜克大学跟你匹配。杜克大学有什么独特的地方吸引你？请写一或两段内容。
分析
杜克大学的学生有一个共性，那就是对学习的热爱。如果你问他们下学期选什么课程，或是目前为止最喜欢的科目，或是最喜欢的教授，抑或是还想深入学习的课本，他们都有一个确定的回答。虽然答案有可能完全不同，但是他们都有自己的回答。这在其他大学里是不常见的，所以这就是为什么这一类的学生都来了杜克大学。
虽然这样说，但并不是建议你在回答里面说“I love to learn”（我喜爱学习）这样的话。这样的话就太简单了。成功的申请者一定是花时间考虑到了他们对学习的渴求。他们会考虑到在高中的学术投入，而这点就能反映出他们对知识的渴求。同时，他们也会考虑杜克大学是如何满足他们的学习欲望的。
对学习的渴望并不是打动录取委员会的唯一利器，但却最能说明你的优点！
范文
为了学到更多并拓展我的兴趣，我加入了由北师大资助的“高中生测试前的焦虑和压力综合征”的研究项目。教授在研究中使用的心理理论和技术能够帮助我们理解人们的思想、感觉和行为。
"What are they thinking?" I am intrigued by this question. When I was young, I answered it using my instinct. As I grew older, I found that instinct was not enough to satisfy my curiosity. To learn more and explore my interests, I joined the Beijing Normal University sponsored research on Pre-test Anxiety and Stress Syndromes among High School Students. I discovered that specific psychological theories and techniques that professors use during the research can help us to understand people's thoughts, feelings and behaviors.
除此之外，我还做过不同方面的研究，用以积累必要的信息来测试假设。在研究过程中，我越来越了解心理这个奇妙的世界。
I was also involved in many aspect of research as to amass necessary information to test some of our hypothesis. I found that during the research and practice, I stepped closer to the magical world of psychology.
杜克大学能够提供给我机会让我去进行独立研究，在里面没有界限，我们能在学术的海洋里遨游。我渴望通过四年的时间在这个校园里学习、成长、成熟和发展。
Duke provides me with the chances to pursue my interest in the independent studies and researches. By participating in Independent Study Research, I can work with faculty members and learn from them and get more additional guidance and supervision. With the same interest I share with the professor, I can have the chance to get more meaningful educational experience during research and communications. So in my future psychology or experimental testing at Duke, I can participate and conduct more researches with more guidance and learn more. I believe this is the future for my studies in the field of psychology in Duke, while boundaries no longer hold us in, departmental walls melt down and we enjoy greatest academic exposures. I love Duke and its encouragement for students to do independent learning and researches, and I aspire to learn, grow, mature and develop through the four years in this campus.
分析
这篇文章的作者极好地理解了杜克大学。诚如上文题目分析里说的那样，对学术的热爱是杜克大学学生的共同点。而这篇文章恰好通过对心理方面的研究表明了这一点。文章一二段讲述作者在心理学方面的研究并对这一领域的喜爱，而第三段讲的是杜克大学在这方面的成就，极好地跟自己的愿望匹配：“So in my future psychology or experimental testing at Duke, I can participate and conduct more researches with more guidance and learn more.”
整篇文章中作者提到的理由只有一个。但这正是作者高明之处。针对Why choose this school? 这类问题而言，不是答得越多越好，而是要答到点上，答到对方的心里。
