## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Brown's mission is to serve the community, the nation and the world by discovering, communicating and preserving knowledge and understanding in a spirit of free inquiry, and by educating and preparing students to discharge the offices of life with usefulness and reputation.

> **Source**: https://www.brown.edu/about/mission

### Values
- **Pursuit of Knowledge and Understanding**: Primary way the University serves community, nation, and world through research and creative expression
- **Academic Freedom and Freedom of Expression**: Freedom to investigate and learn without interference; right to communicate ideas and engage in reasoned protest and dissent
- **Openness and Diversity of Ideas**: Commitment to wide range of ideas, perspectives, and experiences to advance knowledge
- **Responsibility for Thriving Community**: Generating conditions for every individual member to contribute to the University mission

> **Source**: https://policy.brown.edu/policy/values-and-voice

## 六、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **Brown录取率约5.4%**，是八大藤校中较高的
- **亚裔比例较高**：新生约27%，本科生约22%
- **标准化成绩高**：SAT 25th percentile达1510
- **Test-Optional政策**：约40%学生提交标准化成绩
- **开放的课程体系**：Open Curriculum吸引众多申请者

### 趋势

- 申请人数略有下降（-4.7%），但竞争依然激烈
- 亚裔新生比例略有下降（从34.7%到27.3%）
