## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
New York University's mission is to provide an environment of intellectual inquiry and innovation that fosters the development of individuals who will contribute to society as engaged citizens and leaders.

> **Source**: https://www.nyu.edu/about.html

### Values
- **Intellectual Inquiry and Innovation**: Providing environment for groundbreaking research and creative thinking
- **Diversity of Staff and Communities**: Valuing diversity of staff and the communities served
- **Inclusion as Responsibility**: Believing inclusion is both an individual and organizational responsibility
- **Fairness, Integrity, Honesty, Compassion, Respect**: Running processes with these core principles

> **Source**: https://library.nyu.edu/about/general/values/

## 四、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **NYU录取率约9.2%**，国际生比例极高
- **亚裔比例较高**：新生约27%，本科生约22%
- **国际生比例极高**：新生约25%，本科生约26%
- **女生多于男生**：约59%申请者为女生
- **规模大**：本科生约29,000人

### 学校特色

- **地理位置优越**：纽约市曼哈顿
- **Stern商学院**：全美顶尖商学院
- **Tisch艺术学院**：全美顶尖艺术学院
- **法学院**：全美前20
- **国际化程度高**：全球多地有分校
