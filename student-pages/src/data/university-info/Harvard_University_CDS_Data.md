## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
The mission of Harvard College is to educate the citizens and citizen-leaders for our society. We do this through our commitment to the transformative power of a liberal arts and sciences education. Beginning in the classroom with exposure to new ideas, new ways of understanding, and new ways of knowing, students embark on a journey of intellectual transformation. Through a diverse living environment, where students live with people who are studying different topics, who come from different walks of life and have evolving identities, intellectual transformation is deepened and conditions for social transformation are created.

### Vision
Harvard College sets the standard for residential liberal arts and sciences education. We have committed to creating and sustaining the conditions that enable all Harvard College students to experience an unparalleled educational journey that is intellectually, socially, and personally transformative.

> **Source**: https://college.harvard.edu/about/mission-vision-history

### Values
- **Respect**: Respect the rights, differences and dignity of others.
- **Integrity**: Demonstrate honesty and integrity in all dealings.
- **Excellence**: Pursue excellence conscientiously in one's work.
- **Accountability**: Be accountable for actions and conduct in the community.
- **Community**: Cultivate bonds and bridges that enable all to grow and learn from one another.

> **Source**: https://ccl.harvard.edu/
