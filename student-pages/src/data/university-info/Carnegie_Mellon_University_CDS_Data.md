## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Carnegie Mellon University is a global research university noted for its world-class, interdisciplinary education and research. Our mission is to solve real-world problems through innovation and collaboration.

> **Source**: https://www.cmu.edu/about/

### Values
- **Dedication**: Distinctive work ethic and commitment to excellence
- **Impact**: Commitment to address critical issues facing society regionally, nationally, and globally
- **Collaboration**: Interdisciplinary approach, focus on internal and external partnerships
- **Creativity**: Openness to new ideas, intellectual curiosity, willingness to take risks, entrepreneurial spirit
- **Empathy and Compassion**: Focus on improving human condition and personal development of community members
- **Inclusion**: Culture and climate that seeks, welcomes, and advances talented minds from diverse backgrounds
- **Integrity**: Adherence to highest ethical standards in personal and professional behavior

> **Source**: https://www.cmu.edu/about/vision-mission-values

## 五、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **CMU录取率约11.7%**，在顶尖私立大学中相对较高
- **亚裔比例极高**：新生达39.3%，本科生达32.6%，是所有学校中最高的之一
- **标准化成绩极高**：SAT 25th percentile达1510
- **国际生比例高**：新生约20%，本科生约23%
- **STEM强校**：计算机、工程学院全美顶尖

### 学校特色

- **CS/Engineering顶尖**：计算机科学排名全美第一
- **艺术学院**：戏剧学院排名全美前三
- **商学院的Tepper School**：全美顶尖商学院
- **多元文化**：学生群体非常多元化
