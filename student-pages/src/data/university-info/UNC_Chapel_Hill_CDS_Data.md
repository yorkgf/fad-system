## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill is committed to freedom of inquiry and expression, academic excellence, diversity, and public service. Our mission is to teach, to conduct research, and to provide public service.

> **Source**: https://www.unc.edu/about/mission/

### Values
- **Lux, Libertas (Light and Liberty)**: Founding principles guiding the university
- **Excellence**: Unwavering commitment to excellence as one of world's great research universities
- **Leading Change**: Charting bold course to improve society and solve world's greatest problems
- **Equitable Access**: Every individual should have same access to opportunities, be treated equitably and feel valued
- **Diversity as Strength**: Celebrating diversity as source of strength, welcoming different backgrounds and perspectives

> **Source**: https://www.unc.edu/about/mission/

## 四、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **UNC录取率约15.3%**，公立常春藤
- **白人学生为主**：新生约53%，本科生约53%
- **亚裔比例中等**：新生约18.5%，本科生约16%
- **州内生优势**：约83%新生来自北卡州

### 学校特色

- **公立常春藤**：美国最古老的公立大学之一
- **Kenan-Flagler商学院**：全美前20商学院
- **医学院**：全美前10
- **新闻学院**：全美前10
- **Gillings公共卫生学院**：全美前5
