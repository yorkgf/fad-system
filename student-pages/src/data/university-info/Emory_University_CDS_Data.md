## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Emory University is committed to creating, preserving, and teaching knowledge through research, scholarship, and education. We seek to serve humanity by fostering intellectual growth and ethical leadership.

> **Source**: https://www.emory.edu/home/about/index.html

### Values
- **Integrity**: Highest degree of integrity expected of all community members
- **Wise Stewardship**: Exercising wise stewardship over natural and fiscal resources for common good
- **Dignity and Rights**: Upholding dignity and rights of all persons through fair treatment, honest dealing, and respect
- **Collegiality**: Fostering collegiality to advance mission of teaching, research, service, and health care
- **Accountability**: Each member assumes responsibility for actions and holds others accountable
- **Respect and Creativity, Critique, Collaboration**: Employing these in providing courageous leadership

> **Source**: https://ethicsandcompliance.emory.edu/about/values-culture.html

## 六、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **Emory录取率约10.3%**，相对较高
- **亚裔比例较高**：新生约24%，本科生约25%
- **标准化成绩高**：SAT 25th percentile达1480
- **国际生比例较高**：新生约17%，本科生约17%
- **女生申请多于男生**：约60%申请者为女生

### 学校特色

- **N HSC认证**：Goizueta商学院全美顶尖
- **医学预科强校**：健康相关学科排名靠前
- **博雅教育传统**：Oxford College提供两年制文理学院体验
- **地理位置优越**：亚特兰大，机会众多
