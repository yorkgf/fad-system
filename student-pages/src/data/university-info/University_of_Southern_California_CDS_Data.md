## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
The University of Southern California is dedicated to the development of human beings and society as a whole through the cultivation of human understanding, the advancement of knowledge, and the education of students to become responsible contributors to society.

> **Source**: https://www.usc.edu/about/

### Values
- **Integrity**: "We do the right thing" - Words, decisions and actions align with values, honesty and ethical principles
- **Excellence**: "We bring our best selves" - Striving to better ourselves as learning community
- **Well-Being**: Embracing vibrant community and promoting well-being for all Trojans
- **Open Communication**: Engaging in open communication and being accountable for living our values
- **Support**: Supporting fellow Trojans in pursuit of their goals and helping them live by their values
- **Respectful Dialogue**: Actively listening, communicating and providing opportunities for respectful dialogue across all viewpoints

> **Source**: https://usc.edu/we-are-usc/the-university/values/

## 六、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **录取率稳定**：10.02% → 9.81%（微降），竞争仍非常激烈
- **⚠️ 之前记录的4.79%录取率为错误数据**（误将男生录取数3,928当作总录取数）
- **实际总录取数8,050**（男生3,928 + 女生4,117 + 其他5）
- **报到率下降**：44.89% → 43.35%
- **规模大**：本科生约21,163人
- **Test-Optional政策**：SAT提交率仅30%，ACT仅12%

### 学生群体变化

- **西班牙裔比例增长最快**：本科生从18.28%增至22.70%（+4.42%）
- **亚裔比例下降**：本科生从24.78%降至22.53%（-2.25%）
- **白人比例下降**：从27.58%降至24.68%
- **国际生比例下降**：从13.73%降至12.83%
- **⚠️ 种族未知比例大增**：新生从1.6%增至13.81%，需关注数据口径变化

### 学术表现

- **SAT Math极强**：25th-75th 740-790
- **ACT Composite稳定**：32-35
- **平均GPA 3.80**（提交率近100%）
- **保留率95.6%**，六年毕业率92%
