## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Yale is committed to improving the world today and for future generations through outstanding research and scholarship, education, preservation, and practice. Yale educates aspiring leaders worldwide who serve all sectors of society.

> **Source**: https://www.yale.edu/about-yale/mission-statement

### Values
- **Improving the World**: Commitment to outstanding research, scholarship, education, preservation, and practice for future generations
- **Leadership Development**: Educating aspiring leaders who serve all sectors of society
- **Free Exchange of Ideas**: Carrying out mission through open intellectual discourse

> **Source**: https://www.yale.edu/about-yale/mission-statement
