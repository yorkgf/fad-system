## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Vanderbilt University is a private research university founded in 1873. The University provides an environment of excellence in teaching and research that fosters the intellectual, social, and personal development of its students.

> **Source**: https://www.vanderbilt.edu/about/

### Values
- **Excellence**: Committed to excellence in all aspects of work, free of political criteria
- **Truth Through Reason**: Pursuit of truth solely through reason, argument, and evidence rooted in free inquiry
- **Respect**: Valuing multitude of ideas and perspectives from people of all backgrounds
- **Belonging, Self-Direction, Collaboration, Growth**: Core ethos guiding the university community

> **Source**: https://www.vanderbilt.edu/principles/
