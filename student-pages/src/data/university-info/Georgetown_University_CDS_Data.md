## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Georgetown University is a community of communities, committed to academic excellence and the formation of persons dedicated to the service of others. Our mission is rooted in our Jesuit and Catholic identity.

> **Source**: https://governance.georgetown.edu/mission-statement/

### Values
- **Cura Personalis**: Profound care for the whole person and their unique circumstances, gifts, and possibilities
- **Community in Diversity**: Affirming and promoting rich diversity of faith traditions, racial, ethnic, and gender identities
- **Social Justice**: Commitment to inclusive Catholic and Jesuit values for social justice
- **Lifelong Learning**: Educating reflective lifelong learners
- **Civic Engagement**: Forming responsible and active participants in civic life
- **Generous Service**: Living generously in service to others

> **Source**: https://www.georgetown.edu/who-we-are/

## 六、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **录取率稳定**：13.08% → 12.92%（微降 0.16%）
- **申请量增长 2.5%**：从 25,485 增至 26,131
- **报到率下降**：47.96% → 46.68%（-1.28%）
- **州外申请者占主导**：约 85% 申请来自州外和国际
- **国际生录取率低**：约 7-8%，远低于州外申请者

### 学生群体变化

- **白人比例下降**：新生从 52.35% 降至 47.68%（-4.67%）
- **亚裔比例微降**：新生从 18.57% 降至 17.52%
- **种族未知比例大增**：从 3.31% 增至 9.52%（+6.21%）
- **多种族比例增长**：从 7.13% 增至 8.89%

### 学术表现

- **SAT 中位数 1490**，25th-75th 分位 1400-1540
- **ACT 中位数 33**，25th-75th 分位 31-35
- **SAT 提交率 78%**，ACT 提交率 30%
- **GPA 数据未报告**
