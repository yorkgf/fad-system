## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Georgia Tech develops leaders in technology and improves lives through research, innovation, and service. Our mission is to advance human welfare through technological innovation and education.

> **Source**: https://www.gatech.edu/about

### Values
- **Students First**: Educators first and foremost, committed to developing leaders who advance technology and improve human condition
- **Excellence**: Striving to be among the best; setting high expectations for teaching, research, and operations
- **Diversity**: Seeing diversity of backgrounds and perspectives as essential to learning, discovery, and creation
- **Collaboration**: Enabling and celebrating collaboration across disciplines; valuing contributions of all community members
- **Innovation**: Inspiring, empowering, and providing resources for innovative ideas and solutions to flourish
- **Freedom of Inquiry**: Protecting freedom to ask questions, seek truth, and express views
- **Well-Being**: Building healthy and vibrant environment for holistic growth
- **Ethical Conduct**: Holding one another to highest standards of professional and ethical conduct

> **Source**: https://www.gatech.edu/about/values

## 六、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **竞争持续加剧**：录取率从16.5%降至11.1%（三年累计下降5.4个百分点）
- **申请量波动显著**：2024-25学年达峰值（59,789），2025-26骤降25.5%
- **报到率稳步提升**：43.6% → 47.4%（学生认可度增强）
- **公立理工强校**：录取率高于Ivy League但低于UC Berkeley（11.04%）

### 学生群体变化

- **亚裔比例连续下降**：本科生从34.0%降至30.3%（可能因多元化招生策略）
- **国际生比例回升**：2025-26学年达12.0%（前两年约8.4-8.9%）
- **白人占主导**：约47%（稳定但微降）
- **西班牙裔/非裔比例稳定**：合计约10%

### 学术表现

- **SAT/ACT成绩高位稳定**：2024年起SAT中位数显著提升（1370-1530）
- **GPA持续上升**：4.14 → 4.17（学术要求严格）
- **班级排名重视度提升**：2024-25起Top 10%占比达88.7%
- **规模适中**：本科生约21,000人（大型公立理工院校）
