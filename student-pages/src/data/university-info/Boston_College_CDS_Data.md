## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Boston College, a Catholic and Jesuit university, is committed to the pursuit of truth, the integration of knowledge and faith, and the formation of men and women for others. Our mission is to educate students in the liberal arts and sciences.

> **Source**: https://www.bc.edu/about.html

### Values
- **Learn**: Rooted in worldview that calls us to learn and grow intellectually
- **Search for Truth**: Commitment to pursuing truth through faith and reason
- **Live in Service to Others**: Formation of men and women for others through service
- **Look Inward, Reach Out**: Developing minds and talents to fullest and using them in service
- **Dialogue, Exploration, Faith**: Prizing dialogue, exploration, faith, and religious practice

> **Source**: https://www.bc.edu/bc-web/about/mission.html

## 四、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **Boston College录取率约16.2%**，相对较高
- **白人学生为主**：新生约53%，本科生约58%
- **亚裔比例中等**：新生约12%，本科生约11%
- **耶稣会传统**：天主教背景大学
- **女生多于男生**：约59%申请者为女生

### 学校特色

- **Jesuit教育**：耶稣会传统，注重全人教育
- **Carroll管理学院**：全美前30商学院
- ** Lynch护理学院**：全美顶尖护理学院
- **波士顿地理位置**：教育资源丰富
- **毕业率高**：91%+毕业率
