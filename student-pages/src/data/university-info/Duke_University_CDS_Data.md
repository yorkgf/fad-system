## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Duke University seeks to engage the mind, elevate the spirit, and stimulate the best effort of all who are associated with the University; to contribute in diverse ways to the local community, the state, the nation and the world; and to attain and maintain a place of real leadership in all that we do.

> **Source**: https://trustees.duke.edu/mission-statement/

### Values
- **Respect**: Affirm and encourage open expression, academic freedom, regard for others and respect for laws and regulations.
- **Trust**: Be honest, credible and reliable in words, behaviors and actions.
- **Inclusion**: Welcome and fully engage people of diverse backgrounds, abilities and perspectives.
- **Discovery**: Be an innovative community, improve individual capabilities and share knowledge with one another.
- **Excellence**: Perform high quality education, research, patient care and community enhancement with safety and continuous improvement.

> **Source**: https://values.duke.edu/

## 七、GPA数据说明

**重要说明**：Duke University 在CDS中**未公开**以下数据：
- ❌ 高中GPA平均分
- ❌ GPA区间分布
- ❌ AP/IB成绩数据
- ❌ TOEFL/IELTS成绩数据

这是因为：
1. Duke 实行"test-optional"政策（2024-25年约52%学生未提交SAT/ACT）
2. Duke 不强制要求GPA数据
3. 国际学生英语能力数据由ISSO单独统计，未在CDS中公开

## 九、趋势分析

### 录取竞争趋势

| 学年 | 申请人数 | 录取率 |
|------|---------|--------|
| 2023-24 | 46,333 | ~6.8% |
| 2024-25 | 51,795 | ~5.7% |

**趋势**：申请人数大幅增加，但录取名额减少，竞争加剧

| 学年 | SAT提交率 | ACT提交率 |
