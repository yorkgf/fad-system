## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
The University of Chicago values free expression, diversity, and the promotion of the well-being of our neighbors in Hyde Park and the South Side of Chicago.

> **Source**: https://www.uchicago.edu/who-we-are/what-we-value

### Values
- **Freedom of Thought**: Empower individuals to challenge conventional thinking in pursuit of original ideas.
- **Diversity**: Cultivate a wide range of thoughts from peers, students, and communities to bring forth ideas that change the world.
- **Free and Open Discourse**: Foster an unfettered exchange of ideas through free speech and open debate.
- **Intellectual Rigor**: Teach students how to think, not what to think, through rigorous debate and inquiry.

> **Source**: https://www.uchicago.edu/who-we-are/what-we-value
