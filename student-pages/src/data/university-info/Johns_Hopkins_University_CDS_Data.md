## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
The mission of The Johns Hopkins University is to educate its students and cultivate their capacity for life-long learning, to foster independent and original research, and to bring the benefits of discovery to the world.

> **Source**: https://finance.jhu.edu/our-organization/jhu-mission-statement/

### Values
- **Life-Long Learning**: Educating students and cultivating their capacity for continuous learning
- **Independent and Original Research**: Fostering innovative and groundbreaking research
- **Discovery for Global Benefit**: Bringing the benefits of discovery to the world
- **Excellence**: Tireless pursuit of excellence in all endeavors
- **Inclusive Environment**: Fostering passionate teams to develop dynamic business solutions

> **Source**: https://www.jhu.edu/about/history/

## 五、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **JHU录取率约6.4%**，是知名研究型大学中竞争较激烈的
- **亚裔比例极高**：新生达40.8%，本科生29.3%
- **标准化成绩高**：SAT 25th percentile达1530
- **国际生比例较高**：新生约15%，本科生约15%
- **州外生为主**：大部分学生来自Out-of-State
