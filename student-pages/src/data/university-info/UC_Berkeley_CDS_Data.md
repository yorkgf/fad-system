## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
UC Berkeley's mission is to educate students and conduct research that serves the public good. We are committed to excellence, diversity, and the advancement of knowledge.

### Vision
Our vision is to be the world's leading public university, creating a better future through education, research, and public service.

### Values
- **Free Speech**: Commitment to free expression and open discourse
- **Equitable Access**: Ensuring access to education for all qualified students
- **Impact**: Contributing to a better world through knowledge and research
- **Critical Inquiry**: Passion for critical inquiry, debate, discovery, and innovation
- **Contributing to Better World**: Deep commitment to making positive global impact

## 四、标准化考试成绩（SAT/ACT）

### Test-Free政策
- **UC Berkeley实行Test-Free政策**：不使用SAT/ACT成绩进行录取审核
- CDS中明确说明："UC Berkeley is test-free, which means we will not use SAT/ACT test scores in any part of our application process."
- 提交的SAT/ACT成绩仅用于入学后的课程 placement 或学分认定

## 七、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **录取率稳定在11%左右**：11.66% → 11.04% → 11.45%，三年基本持平
- **⚠️ 之前记录的4.19%录取率为错误数据**（误将男生录取数5,198当作总录取数）
- **实际总录取数14,276**（23-24年），录取率11.66%
- **报到率稳定**：46.54% → 45.73% → 46.04%
- **规模大**：本科生约33,000人
- **Test-Free政策**：不使用SAT/ACT成绩进行录取审核

### 学生群体变化

- **亚裔比例稳定**：新生约37-38%，本科生约35%
- **国际生比例下降**：新生从9.19%降至4.80%（三年趋势）
- **西班牙裔比例最高**：约21-23%
- **种族未知比例增加**：从4.20%增至7.37%（25-26年显著增加）

### 学术表现

- **学术GPA非常重要**（Very Important）
- 高中课程难度（Rigor of secondary school record）为最重要因素
- Test-Free政策下，其他学术指标（GPA、AP/IB成绩等）更为关键
