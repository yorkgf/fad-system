## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Rice University's mission is to advance research and education, foster innovations that benefit humanity, and prepare leaders in all fields of human endeavor.

> **Source**: https://www.rice.edu/mission-values

### Values
- **Responsibility**: Take ownership of actions and decisions in all aspects of university life.
- **Integrity**: Uphold honesty and doing what's right, embodied in the Honor Code.
- **Community**: Foster an inclusive learning community that promotes excellence and student success through a culture of care.
- **Excellence**: Pursue groundbreaking research and unsurpassed education for the betterment of the world.

## 七、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **录取率稳定**：7.88% → 8.00%（微升 0.12%）
- **申请量增长 4.6%**：从 31,059 增至 32,473
- **报到率下降**：45.97% → 44.24%（-1.73%）
- **国际生录取率低**：约 3.95%，低于州内/州外
- **规模适中**：本科生约 4,776 人

### 学生群体变化

- **亚裔比例稳定**：新生约 29-30%，本科生约 29%
- **西班牙裔比例增长**：本科生从 16.11% 增至 16.69%
- **白人比例增长**：新生从 23.64% 增至 25.63%
- **非裔比例下降**：新生从 7.85% 降至 5.84%

### 学术表现

- **SAT 中位数 1540**，25th-75th 分位 1510-1560
- **ACT 中位数 35**，25th-75th 分位 34-36
- **SAT 提交率 48-50%**，ACT 提交率 22%
- **GPA 数据未报告**
