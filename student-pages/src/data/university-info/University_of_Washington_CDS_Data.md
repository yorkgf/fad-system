## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
The University of Washington's mission is to create, disseminate, and preserve knowledge. We are committed to educating students and advancing research that benefits society.

> **Source**: https://www.washington.edu/about/

### Values
- **Integrity**: Acting with honesty and ethical engagement in all endeavors
- **Diversity**: Embracing diverse perspectives and backgrounds as essential to learning
- **Excellence**: Recruiting best, most diverse and innovative faculty and staff; holding ourselves to highest standards of ethics
- **Collaboration**: Enabling collaboration across disciplines, units, and with organizations worldwide
- **Innovation**: Fostering culture of innovation, collaboration, and discovery with transformational impact
- **Respect**: Promoting access to excellence and fostering creativity through mutual respect

> **Source**: https://www.washington.edu/about/visionvalues/

## 六、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **录取率下降**：42.52% → 39.14%（竞争加剧）
- **申请量增长 10.8%**：从 62,428 增至 69,166
- **报到率稳定**：26.37% → 26.57%
- **公立大学**：作为华盛顿州旗舰公立大学，州内学生有优势
- **规模大**：本科生约 32,000 人

### 学生群体变化

- **亚裔比例稳定增长**：新生从 26.64% 增至 27.27%
- **国际生比例下降**：从 12.54% 降至 10.75%
- **西班牙裔比例增长**：从 9.34% 增至 11.44%
- **非裔比例增长**：从 3.74% 增至 4.50%

### 学术表现

- **SAT 中位数 1410**（2023-24），25th-75th 分位 1280-1490
- **公立大学中学术水平较高**
