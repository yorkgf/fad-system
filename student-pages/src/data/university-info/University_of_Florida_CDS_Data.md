## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
The University of Florida is a public land-grant research university committed to the discovery and dissemination of knowledge, the preparation of students for lives of productivity and responsible citizenship, and the application of knowledge to benefit the state, nation, and world.

> **Source**: https://www.ufl.edu/about/

### Values
- **Excellence**: Standard is to be the best by doing our best; strive to lead with integrity and distinction
- **Discovery and Innovation**: Enhancing discovery and innovation through growing diversity
- **Inclusion**: Diversity enhances discovery; reliant on freedom and civility; enriches UF community
- **Freedom and Civility**: Rooted in stewardship; connective tissue for all Core Values
- **Community**: Building supportive environment where all can contribute
- **Stewardship**: Show respect for those who came before, responsibility for those now with us, commitment to leave just and habitable world

> **Source**: https://www.ufl.edu/about/core-values/

## 七、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **录取率稳定**：24.03% → 24.20%，基本持平
- **申请量增长12.5%**：从65,375增至73,557
- **报到率偏低**：约42-43%（公立大学正常现象，州内生选择较多）
- **规模庞大**：本科生约35,629人
- **州内生优势明显**：入学学生中76%来自佛罗里达州
- **SAT/ACT强制要求**：提交率80%/40%，成绩是录取因素之一

### 学生群体变化

- **西班牙裔比例最高**：本科生24.64%，新生24.18%
- **亚裔比例稳定增长**：本科生从11.95%增至12.43%
- **白人比例缓慢下降**：从49.99%降至48.86%
- **国际生比例偏低**：本科生仅2.59%

### 学术表现

- **SAT中位数1400**，25th-75th分位1330-1470
- **ACT中位数31**，25th-75th分位29-33
- **平均GPA 3.92**，94%学生提交GPA
- **保留率98%**（2023入学cohort到2024秋季）
- **毕业率98%**（2018入学cohort六年毕业率）
