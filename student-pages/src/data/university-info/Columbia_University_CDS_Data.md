## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
The mission of Columbia College is to equip every Columbia College student with the habits of mind necessary for a life of civic engagement, meaningful achievement and professional success.

### Vision
The highest aim of Columbia College is to provide a transformative undergraduate student experience where academic excellence, community stewardship and personal well-being are mutually enriching and integrally connected in the pursuit of our mission.

### Values
- **Thoughtful, Rigorous Debate**: Commitment to respectful discourse that respects collective rights to learn, work, and live together
- **Freedom from Bigotry**: Free of intimidation and harassment in pursuit of knowledge
- **Diversity as Strength**: Embracing differences as source of strength and innovation
- **Safety and Inclusion**: Ensuring everyone feels safe, seen, welcome, and heard

> **Source**: https://president.columbia.edu/news/our-community-our-values

## 六、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **Columbia录取率约3.9%**，是八大藤校中最低之一
- **亚裔比例较高**：新生约26%，本科生约18%
- **标准化成绩极高**：SAT 25th percentile达1510
- **国际生比例高**：新生约17%，本科生约21%
- **规模大**：本科生约9,164人（含GS项目）

### 学校特色

- **核心课程(Core Curriculum)**：独特的博雅教育体系
- **纽约市地理位置**：得天独厚的城市资源
- **多个本科学院**：Columbia College, Engineering, General Studies
