## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Dartmouth College educates the most promising students and prepares them for a lifetime of learning and of responsible leadership, through a faculty dedicated to teaching and the creation of knowledge.

> **Source**: https://www.dartmouth.edu/jameswright/archive/mission/index.html

### Values
- **Integrity**: Fostering a culture of honesty and ethical conduct
- **Self-Reliance**: Encouraging independence and personal responsibility
- **Collegiality**: Building lasting bonds among faculty, staff, and students
- **Responsibility**: Instilling sense of responsibility for each other and the broader world

> **Source**: https://admissions.dartmouth.edu/dartmouth-values

## 四、标准化考试成绩（SAT/ACT）

### 政策变化
- **2023-24**: Test-Optional（不强制提交）
- **2024-25**: 恢复强制要求（"Test Optional Admissions policy ended with the Fall 2024 application cycle. Standardized testing will be required for the Fall 2025 admissions cycle."）

### SAT/ACT成绩数据
- **两年CDS均未报告具体SAT/ACT分数和提交率**（25th/75th百分位均为空白）
- 23-24年分数区间分布全部为0%
- 24-25年尚未报告分数数据（可能因首次恢复强制要求，数据待后续公布）

## 八、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **录取率下降**：6.23% → 5.40%（竞争加剧）
- **申请增长近10%**：28,841 → 31,656
- **报到率上升**：67.22% → 69.12%
- **Ivy League中规模最小**：本科生约4,474人
- **2024-25恢复SAT/ACT强制要求**，是Ivy中最早恢复的

### 学生群体变化

- **白人比例下降**：本科生从47.45%降至44.10%
- **亚裔比例微降**：从14.30%降至13.10%
- **国际生比例上升**：从14.10%升至14.91%
- **西班牙裔比例稳定**：约9.7-9.9%
- **种族未知比例增加**：从2.06%升至2.99%

### 学术表现

- **高中排名极高**：93%在Top 10%，99%在Top 25%
- SAT/ACT成绩未公开，但2025年起强制要求
- GPA数据未报告
