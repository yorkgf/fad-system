## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
The University of Notre Dame is a Catholic academic community of higher learning under the sponsorship of the Congregation of Holy Cross, animated by the Gospel of Jesus Christ. It seeks to cultivate in its students an integrated habit of thought, life, and action guided by faith, reason, and love.

> **Source**: https://www.nd.edu/about/mission/

### Values
- **Catholic Mission**: Our Catholic mission guides and informs all that we do and how we work together
- **Truth**: Commitment to seeking and upholding truth in all endeavors
- **Community**: Fostering a supportive academic community
- **Service**: Dedicated to serving others and making a positive impact
- **Sustainability**: Commitment to environmental and social sustainability
- **Responsibility and Ownership**: Takes responsibility for decisions, actions, and results

> **Source**: https://www.nd.edu/about/mission/

## 五、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **Notre Dame录取率约11.3%**，相对较高
- **白人学生为主**：新生约59%，本科生约59%
- **亚裔比例较低**：新生约5.6%，本科生约5.7%
- **天主教背景**：传统强校，有独特文化
- **国际生比例**：新生约8%，本科生约7.5%
- **州外生为主**：约95%学生来自Out-of-State

### 学术特点

- **顶尖商学院**：Mendoza商学院排名靠前
- **工程强校**：Corsentino推荐
- **地理位置**：印第安纳州南本德市
