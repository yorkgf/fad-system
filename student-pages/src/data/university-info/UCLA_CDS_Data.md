## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
UCLA's mission is to create, disseminate, preserve and apply knowledge for the betterment of our global society. We pursue this mission through teaching, research, and public service.

> **Source**: https://www.ucla.edu/about/mission-and-values

### Values
- **Open Access to Information**: Valuing free and open access to knowledge and information
- **Free and Lively Debate**: Conducting discourse with mutual respect for individuals
- **Freedom from Intolerance**: Commitment to environment free of intolerance
- **Excellence and Diversity**: Striving for excellence recognizing that openness and inclusion produce true quality

## 六、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **UCLA录取率持续上升**：8.73% → 9.41%（但仍低于10%）
- **竞争激烈**：录取率保持在个位数
- **报到率下降**：从51.7%降至48.0%
- **申请量稳定**：两年申请人数基本持平

### 学生群体变化

- **国际生比例**：8.38%（2025-26）
- **性别录取差异**：女性录取率略高于男性
- **多样性**：国际生占比超过8%

## 七、数据说明

### 可用学年

- **2023-24学年**：完整数据（申请、录取、入学、性别构成）

- SAT/ACT成绩
- GPA数据
- 高中班级排名
