## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Caltech's mission is to expand human knowledge and benefit society through research integrated with education. We investigate the most challenging, fundamental problems in science and technology in a singularly collegial, interdisciplinary atmosphere, while educating outstanding students to become creative members of society.

> **Source**: https://www.eas.caltech.edu/about/strategic-plan

### Values
- **Honor**: Unwavering commitment to honor in all academic and personal endeavors
- **Integrity**: Commitment to ethical conduct and honesty
- **Equity**: Fostering an equitable environment for all community members
- **Diversity**: Valuing each other's different experiences and perspectives
- **Different Experiences and Perspectives**: Cultivating environment where preconceptions are challenged and ideas expanded

> **Source**: https://deans.caltech.edu/HonorCode/statement-community
