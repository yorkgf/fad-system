## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Cornell's mission is to discover, preserve and disseminate knowledge, to educate the next generation of global citizens, and to promote a culture of broad inquiry throughout and beyond the Cornell community.

> **Source**: https://www.cornell.edu/about/mission.cfm

### Values
- **Purposeful Discovery**: Value discovery through learning, teaching, scholarship, and innovation with integrity and excellence.
- **Free and Open Inquiry and Expression**: Commit to free and open inquiry and expression, even of ideas some may consider wrong or offensive.
- **A Community of Belonging**: Strive to be a welcoming, caring, and equitable community where all can learn and work in respect.
- **Exploration across Boundaries**: Celebrate the power of connections among all academic disciplines.
- **Changing Lives through Public Engagement**: Apply knowledge created for the benefit of society through engagement in community, state, and world.
- **Respect for the Natural Environment**: Advance solutions for a sustainable future and recognize the relationship between people and Earth.

> **Source**: https://www.cornell.edu/about/values.cfm

## 六、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **录取率略有上升**：7.9% → 8.41% （申请人数减少但录取人数增加）
- **报到率下降**：66.0% → 63.90% （下降2.10%）
- **竞争依然激烈**：录取率仍低于9%
- **Ivy League中录取率相对较高**（相比Harvard ~3.65%, Princeton ~4.62%）

### 学生群体变化

- **亚裔比例持续增长**：本科生从25.0%增至26.84%
- **国际生比例略有下降**：从10.3%降至9.49%
- **白人比例稳定**：约31%
- **西班牙裔比例下降**：从14.5%降至13.08%

### 学术表现

- **SAT成绩提升明显**：25th percentile从1480升至1510
- **SAT Math表现优异**：75th percentile达800（满分）
- **标准化成绩高**：整体学术水平优秀
- **规模较大**：本科生约15,792人，是Ivy League中规模最大的
