## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Stanford University, through its School of Humanities and Sciences, is committed to creating and sharing knowledge that is open to all and to preparing students to be curious, critical thinkers who contribute to the world.

### Vision
Stanford is committed to creating a future where humans and nature thrive, with ambitious goals for reducing energy, water and waste on campus.

> **Source**: https://www.stanford.edu/about/

### Values
- **Integrity**: Uphold the highest ethical standards in all endeavors.
- **Diversity**: Embrace and celebrate diverse perspectives and backgrounds.
- **Respect**: Treat all members of the community with dignity and consideration.
- **Freedom of Inquiry and Expression**: Foster open intellectual exploration and dialogue.
- **Trust**: Build and maintain trust through honest and transparent actions.
- **Honesty**: Commit to truthfulness in all communications and work.
- **Fairness**: Ensure equitable treatment and opportunities for all.

> **Source**: https://uco.stanford.edu/stanford-values

## 五、趋势分析

### 录取竞争趋势

**趋势**：Stanford录取率持续在3-4%左右，竞争极其激烈

- **亚裔比例极高**：Stanford新生亚裔占比达33%，是所有顶尖名校中最高的
