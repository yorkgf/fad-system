## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
The University of Virginia's mission is to serve the Commonwealth and the world through the discovery, preservation, and dissemination of knowledge, and through the education of students and the professional development of scholars.

> **Source**: https://www.virginia.edu/about/

### Values
- **Honor**: Distinctive foundational value guiding all conduct
- **Integrity**: Acting and communicating honestly with integrity at all times
- **Trust**: Building trust within collaborative, diverse community
- **Respect**: Valuing all members of community by recognizing and honoring similarities and differences
- **Openness, Fairness, Trustworthiness**: Encouraging and celebrating differences, seeking numerous perspectives
- **Excellence and Affordable Access**: Universal dedication to excellence and accessible education

> **Source**: https://www.virginia.edu/mission/

## 六、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **录取率下降**：18.66% → 16.81%（竞争加剧）
- **申请量增长15.8%**：从50,926增至58,951
- **报到率下降**：42.31% → 39.97%
- **州内优势明显**：州内录取率25.93%，州外13.83%，国际10.46%
- **规模适中**：本科生约17,577人（degree-seeking）

### 学生群体变化

- **亚裔比例稳定增长**：本科生从17.91%增至19.93%（+2.02%）
- **白人比例下降**：从52.60%降至48.70%（-3.90%）
- **西班牙裔比例增长**：从7.16%增至7.91%
- **非裔比例略有增长**：从7.06%增至7.50%
- **多样性持续提升**

### 学术表现

- **SAT中位数1470**，25th-75th分位1410-1520
- **ACT中位数33**，25th-75th分位32-35
- **SAT/ACT提交率下降**：SAT从54%降至45.6%，ACT从17%降至13.8%
- **GPA提交率81.47%**
