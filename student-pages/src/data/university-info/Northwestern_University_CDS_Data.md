## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Northwestern is committed to excellent teaching, innovative research and the personal and intellectual growth of its students in a diverse academic community.

> **Source**: https://www.northwestern.edu/about/mission-vision-priorities/

### Values
- **Transform Society**: Commit to making a positive impact on the world through research and education.
- **Grow Leaders**: Develop the next generation of leaders across all fields.
- **Champion Access, Opportunity and Belonging**: Ensure all members of the community can thrive.
- **Encourage Debate**: Foster free speech, academic freedom, and engagement across difference.
- **Embrace Breadth and Depth**: Pursue excellence across all disciplines.
- **Pursue Excellence**: Maintain the highest standards in teaching, research, and service.
- **Strengthen Community**: Build a diverse and inclusive academic community.
- **Care about One Another**: Support the wellbeing and success of all community members.

> **Source**: https://www.northwestern.edu/about/mission-vision-priorities/

## 六、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **Northwestern录取率约7.7%**，是伊利诺伊州顶尖名校
- **亚裔比例较高**：本科生约21%，新生约20%
- **标准化成绩极高**：SAT 25th percentile达1510
- **国际生比例较高**：新生约13%
- **Media强校**：传播学院全美第一

### 学校特色

- **Medill新闻学院**：全美第一的新闻传播学院
- **Kellogg商学院**：全美顶尖商学院
- **工程学院**：全美前15
- **Pritzker法学院**：全美顶尖法学院
- **地理位置优越**：芝加哥附近，埃文斯顿校区
