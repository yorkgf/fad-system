## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
The University of Michigan's mission is to serve the people of Michigan and the world through preeminence in creating, communicating, preserving and applying knowledge, art, and academic values, and in developing leaders and citizens who will challenge the present and enrich the future.

> **Source**: https://president.umich.edu/about/mission/

### Values
- **Integrity**: Acting with honesty and taking responsibility for actions
- **Compassion**: Demonstrating care and empathy for others
- **Hope**: Maintaining optimism and resilience in face of challenges
- **Community**: Building supportive environment where all can thrive
- **Fulfillment**: Growing happiness, meaning, and resilience in our lives
- **Stewardship and Accountability**: Serving as responsible stewards of resources, acting within ethical frameworks

> **Source**: https://culturejourney.umich.edu/values/

## 六、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **录取率稳定在16%左右**：16.64% → 15.64% → 16.42%，三年波动很小
- **申请量持续增长**：94,500 → 98,310 → 109,112（三年增长15.5%）
- **报到率下降**：47.49% → 47.35% → 45.65%
- **规模大**：本科生约34,000人
- **州外申请者占主导**：约65-70%申请来自州外和国际

### 学生群体变化

- **亚裔比例下降后回升**：新生从20.24%降至18.16%再回升至18.71%
- **西班牙裔比例增长**：本科生从9.98%增至11.61%
- **白人比例下降**：本科生从48.63%降至46.49%
- **种族未知比例增加**：新生从3.63%增至6.68%

### 学术表现

- **SAT中位数1460-1470**，25th-75th分位1350-1530
- **ACT中位数33**，25th-75th分位31-34
- **平均GPA 3.90**，提交率92-98%
- **学术GPA为最重要录取因素**（Very Important）
