## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Princeton University advances learning through scholarship, research, and teaching of unsurpassed quality, with an emphasis on undergraduate and doctoral education that is distinctive among the world's great universities, and with a pervasive commitment to serve the nation and the world.

> **Source**: https://www.princeton.edu/meet-princeton/mission-statement

### Values
- **Excellence in Scholarship**: Advancing learning through scholarship, research, and teaching of unsurpassed quality
- **Distinctive Education**: Emphasis on undergraduate and doctoral education among world's great universities
- **Service to Nation and World**: Pervasive commitment to serve society beyond campus
- **Fairness**: A core value guiding all university activities and decisions

> **Source**: https://www.princeton.edu/meet-princeton/mission-statement

## 七、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **录取率极低**：4.49% → 4.62% → 4.42%（Ivy League 中最低之一）
- **申请量持续增长**：39,644 → 40,468 → 42,303（三年增长 6.71%）
- **报到率下降**：76.65% → 75.48% → 75.32%（-1.33%）
- **国际生录取率最低**：约 2.1-2.4%，远低于州内/州外（约 5%）
- **规模最小**：本科生约 5,800 人（Ivy 中最小）

### 学生群体变化

- **新生总数波动**：1,364 → 1,833 → 1,404（24-25 可能统计口径变化）
- **白人比例下降**：本科生从 36.84% 降至 31.35%（-5.49%）
- **亚裔比例稳定**：本科生约 23-25%
- **种族未知比例增加**：从 2.46% 增至 5.40%（+2.94%）

### 学术表现

- **SAT 中位数 1530**，25th-75th 分位 1490-1560（全美最高之一）
- **ACT 中位数 35**，25th-75th 分位 34-35
- **SAT 提交率 60%**，ACT 提交率 20%
- **GPA 分布优秀**：68.5% 为 4.0 满分（2024-25）
