## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
MIT's mission is to advance knowledge and educate students in science, technology, and other areas of scholarship that will best serve the nation and the world in the 21st century. The Institute is committed to generating, disseminating, and preserving knowledge, and to working with others to bring this knowledge to bear on the world's great challenges.

> **Source**: https://www.mit.edu/about/mission-statement/

### Values
- **Excellence and Curiosity**: Strive for the highest standards of integrity, and intellectual and creative excellence. Prize originality, ingenuity, honesty, and boldness.
- **Openness and Respect**: Champion the open sharing of information and ideas. Cherish free expression, debate, and dialogue in pursuit of truth with respect for each other.
- **Belonging and Community**: Make the community a humane and welcoming place where people from diverse backgrounds can grow and thrive. Value potential over pedigree.

> **Source**: https://www.mit.edu/values/

## 六、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **MIT录取率约4.5%**，是全美最难录取的学校之一
- **亚裔比例极高**：新生达42.1%，本科生35.2%，是所有顶尖名校中最高的之一
- **标准化成绩极高**：SAT 98%+学生在1500以上，ACT 99%+学生在34以上
- **STEM强校**：以理工科著称，录取竞争极其激烈
- **规模较小**：本科生约4,500人

### 招生政策

- MIT **要求提交SAT或ACT成绩**用于录取决定
- 非常重视标准化考试成绩
- 高中成绩单、推荐信、课外活动等也是重要因素
