## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
The University of Texas at Austin is dedicated to the discovery, dissemination, and preservation of knowledge through teaching, research, and creative activity. We prepare students to become leaders in their fields and engaged citizens.

> **Source**: https://www.utexas.edu/about

### Values
- **Learning**: A caring community, all students helping one another grow
- **Discovery**: Expanding knowledge and human understanding
- **Freedom**: To seek the truth and express it
- **Leadership**: The will to excel with integrity and the spirit that nothing is impossible
- **Individual Opportunity**: Many options, diverse people and ideas, one University
- **Responsibility**: To serve as catalyst for positive change in Texas and beyond

> **Source**: https://www.utexas.edu/about/mission-and-values

## 四、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **UT Austin录取率约26.6%**，德州学生有显著优势
- **亚裔比例较高**：新生约26%，本科生约26%
- **多元化**：西班牙裔和白人学生比例相近
- **州内生优势**：约59%新生来自德州

### 学校特色

- **顶尖公立大学**：德州大学旗舰校区
- **McCombs商学院**：全美前10商学院
- **工程学院**：全美前15
- **计算机科学**：全美前10
- **体育强校**：Big 12联盟成员
