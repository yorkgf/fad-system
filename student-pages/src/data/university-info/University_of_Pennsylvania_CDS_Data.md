## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
The University of Pennsylvania is a scholarly community that advances discovery and opportunity toward a better future for all. We embrace excellence, freedom of inquiry and expression, and respect.

> **Source**: https://www.upenn.edu/about/university-values

### Values
- **Excellence**: Embrace excellence in all scholarly pursuits.
- **Freedom of Inquiry and Expression**: Champion open intellectual exploration and dialogue.
- **Respect**: Treat all members of the community with dignity and consideration.

> **Source**: https://www.upenn.edu/about/university-values
