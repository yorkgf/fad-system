## School Mission & Vision

### Mission
Washington University in St. Louis is dedicated to the discovery and dissemination of knowledge and to the personal growth and development of our students. Through research, scholarship, and creative expression, we seek to enhance the human condition.

> **Source**: https://wustl.edu/about/mission-statement/

### Values
- **Excellence**: Hold ourselves to the highest standards in teaching, research, scholarship, patient care and service.
- **Inclusivity**: Welcome students, faculty and staff from all backgrounds to create a nurturing and intellectually rigorous community.
- **Lifelong Learning**: Cultivate habits of lifelong learning and critical and ethical thinking in students.
- **Innovation**: Encourage faculty and students to be innovative, bold, independent, critical thinkers.
- **Ethical Principles**: Build an inclusive, equitable, respectful, ethically-principled environment for living, teaching, learning and working.
- **Community Impact**: Contribute positively to our home community and effect meaningful, constructive change in the world.

> **Source**: https://wustl.edu/about/mission-statement/

## 七、特点分析

### 录取特点

- **录取率稳定**：11.96% → 12.06%（微升 0.10%）
- **申请量增长 1.6%**：从 32,240 增至 32,754
- **报到率下降**：47.42% → 46.75%（-0.67%）
- **规模适中**：本科生约 7,857 人

### 学生群体变化

- **亚裔比例稳定**：新生约 22-23%，本科生约 21%
- **白人比例增长**：新生从 35.67% 增至 37.14%
- **种族未知比例大增**：从 0.98% 增至 5.20%（+4.22%）
- **非裔比例下降**：从 8.00% 降至 5.74%

### 学术表现

- **SAT 中位数 1540**，25th-75th 分位 1500-1570
- **ACT 中位数 34**，25th-75th 分位 33-35
- **SAT 提交率 29%**，ACT 提交率 28%
- **GPA 数据未报告**
